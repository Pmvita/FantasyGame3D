# Magic Swordsman

**Role:** Ultimate Hybrid Warrior / Transcendent Fighter  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (All paths)

## Description

Magic Swordsmen are the ultimate warriors, having transcended the boundaries between physical combat and magic. These legendary fighters have achieved perfect fusion of blade and spell, wielding enchanted weapons with devastating magical enhancements. Magic Swordsmen represent the absolute peak of warrior evolution, capable of adapting to any situation through perfect balance of might and magic.

## Skills

### 1. Arcane Blade Mastery
**Type:** Weapon Enhancement / Versatility  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect mastery over magical blades for 60 seconds. Your weapon adapts to deal the most effective damage type against each enemy. All attacks deal both physical and magical damage simultaneously, and you can switch between all elemental damage types at will.

### 2. Spell-Sword Fusion
**Type:** Combination Attack  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Fuse a spell with a sword strike, creating unique combination attacks. Each combination has unique effects: Fire + Slash creates a burning arc, Ice + Thrust freezes enemies, Lightning + Swing chains between targets. Unlimited combinations possible.

### 3. Reality Rend
**Type:** Ultimate Attack  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Cut through reality itself with magic-enhanced blade, dealing 1000% weapon damage plus maximum magical damage to all enemies in a line. The cut persists for 10 seconds, damaging enemies who pass through it. Ignores all defenses.

### 4. Adaptive Mastery
**Type:** Universal Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect adaptation for 90 seconds. During this time, you automatically counter any attack type (physical, magical, ranged), can use any warrior or mage ability regardless of class restrictions, and all your abilities deal 100% more damage. Ultimate versatility.

### 5. Transcendence: Unity of Blade and Spell
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Transcend mortal limitations, becoming a being of pure blade and magic energy for 120 seconds. While transformed, you are immune to all damage, can use all abilities from all classes simultaneously, and deal 500% more damage. All attacks are instant-cast and have no cooldowns. Ultimate power that transcends all class boundaries.
