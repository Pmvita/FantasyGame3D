# Dragon Knight

**Role:** Dragon Transformer / Elemental Dominator  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Dragon Knights are legendary warriors who have bonded with ancient dragons, gaining the ability to transform into powerful dragon forms. These transcendent fighters excel at form-based combat, switching between human and dragon forms to dominate the battlefield. Dragon Knights have massive presence, commanding respect and fear through their draconic might. Their elemental dominance makes them masters of any battlefield.

## Skills

### 1. Dragon Form
**Type:** Transformation / Elemental Mastery  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into your bonded dragon form for 90 seconds. While in dragon form, you gain flight, massive health increase (300%), elemental breath attacks, and immunity to crowd control. Different dragons provide different elemental affinities (fire, ice, lightning, poison). Massive presence on the battlefield.

### 2. Elemental Breath
**Type:** Area Damage / Elemental  
**Mana Cost:** 60 (In Dragon Form)  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a devastating breath attack in a cone, dealing massive elemental damage based on your dragon type. Fire breath burns, ice breath freezes, lightning chains, poison corrupts. Each breath attack has unique secondary effects. Can only be used in dragon form.

### 3. Dragon's Roar
**Type:** Area Debuff / Fear  
**Mana Cost:** 70 (In Dragon Form)  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a terrifying roar that fears all enemies in a large area for 10 seconds and reduces their damage by 50% for 30 seconds. The roar also buffs all allies, increasing their damage by 25% for 30 seconds. Massive presence ability.

### 4. Draconic Scales
**Type:** Defensive / Elemental Resistance  
**Mana Cost:** 50 (In Dragon Form)  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Your draconic scales harden for 45 seconds, reducing all damage taken by 60% and reflecting 40% of physical damage back to attackers. Elemental damage matching your dragon type heals you instead of damaging. Ultimate dragon defense.

### 5. True Dragon Form
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 200  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Achieve perfect unity with your dragon, transforming into your true draconic form for 180 seconds. While transformed, you are a true dragon: massive size, flight, all elemental breath attacks available, and complete elemental dominance. All enemies take 50% more damage from all sources while in your presence. You become a walking apocalypse with massive battlefield presence.
