# Gladiator

**Role:** Arena Specialist / Duelist  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Gladiators are warriors who have mastered one-on-one combat through arena training. These skilled duelists excel at fighting single opponents, using precise techniques and crowd-pleasing moves. Gladiators are highly effective in PvP and single-target encounters, combining offense and defense in perfect balance.

## Skills

### 1. Colosseum Strike
**Type:** High Single Target Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Execute a perfect strike that deals 300% weapon damage to a single target. Damage is increased by 50% if the target is attacking you. Shows respect for worthy opponents.

### 2. Arena Presence
**Type:** Buff / Threat Generation  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Command the arena's attention for 30 seconds, increasing all damage by 25% and making enemies more likely to target you. Your presence inspires allies, granting them 10% increased damage.

### 3. Perfect Guard
**Type:** Defensive / Counter  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a perfect defensive stance for 5 seconds. While active, automatically block the next 3 attacks and counter-attack for 250% weapon damage. Cannot be bypassed by normal attacks.

### 4. Finishing Move
**Type:** Execute / High Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Execute a devastating finishing move that deals 500% weapon damage to targets below 40% health. If the target dies, all your cooldowns are reduced by 20 seconds. Theatrical and effective.

### 5. Champion's Resolve
**Type:** Ultimate Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve champion status for 60 seconds, increasing all stats by 30% and making you immune to crowd control. While active, defeating an enemy restores 30% health and extends the duration by 10 seconds. Ultimate arena dominance.
