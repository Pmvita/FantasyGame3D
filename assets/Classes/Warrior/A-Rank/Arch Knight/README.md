# Arch Knight

**Role:** Elite Tank / Party Protector  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (Knight path)

## Description

Arch Knights are the pinnacle of defensive warriors, having mastered the art of protection to perfection. These elite tanks excel at mitigating damage for themselves and their party, using advanced defensive techniques and tactical awareness. Arch Knights are highly sought after for challenging group content due to their unparalleled survivability and threat management.

## Skills

### 1. Iron Fortress
**Type:** Ultimate Defense  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Become an immovable fortress for 12 seconds, reducing all damage taken by 70% and reflecting 30% of damage back to attackers. Cannot be moved by enemies during this time.

### 2. Guardian's Resolve
**Type:** Threat / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of absolute determination, generating massive threat and reducing all party member damage taken by 15% for 15 seconds. Your defensive stats are shared with nearby allies.

### 3. Shield of Retribution
**Type:** Counter / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Prepare your shield for the next incoming attack. When struck, reflect 200% of the damage back and stun the attacker for 3 seconds. Lasts 5 seconds or until triggered.

### 4. Rallying Stand
**Type:** Party Buff / Cooldown  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Plant your banner, creating an area that grants all allies within 20 meters 25% damage reduction and immunity to crowd control for 10 seconds. You are immune to all damage while the banner stands.

### 5. Last Stand
**Type:** Survival / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Cooldown:** 240 seconds  
**Description:** When health drops below 10%, automatically activate Last Stand, preventing death for 8 seconds. During this time, you cannot drop below 1 health but take 50% more damage. Must survive to benefit from healing.
