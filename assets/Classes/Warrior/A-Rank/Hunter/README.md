# Hunter

**Role:** Ranged Physical DPS / Pet Master  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (Thief path)

## Description

Hunters are masters of ranged combat and wilderness survival, using bows, crossbows, and trained beasts to overcome their enemies. These skilled marksmen excel at dealing sustained damage from range while maintaining excellent mobility. Hunters can tame and command various pets, adding versatility to their combat approach.

## Skills

### 1. Aimed Shot
**Type:** High Damage / Channel  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Aim carefully for 2 seconds, then fire a devastating shot that deals 300% weapon damage. Critical hit chance increased by 50% when fully channeled.

### 2. Beast Companion
**Type:** Pet Summon / Passive  
**Mana Cost:** 50 (Initial)  
**Cooldown:** None (Pet persists)  
**Description:** Summon a loyal beast companion that fights alongside you. The pet deals damage and can be commanded to attack specific targets. Different beasts offer different abilities (tank, DPS, or utility).

### 3. Trap Mastery
**Type:** Crowd Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Place a trap on the ground that activates when enemies step on it. Can be frost (slows), fire (damage over time), or snare (roots). Up to 3 traps can be active at once.

### 4. Multi-Shot
**Type:** Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Fire arrows at up to 5 nearby enemies simultaneously, dealing 120% weapon damage to each. Effective for handling multiple targets.

### 5. Aspect of the Hawk
**Type:** Self-Buff / Mobility  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enter hawk form for 20 seconds, increasing ranged damage by 30% and movement speed by 40%. While active, attacks have a chance to reset trap cooldowns.
