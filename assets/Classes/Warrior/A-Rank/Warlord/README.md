# Warlord

**Role:** Tactical Leader / Support Warrior  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Warlords are battlefield commanders who excel at leading groups to victory through tactical prowess and inspiring presence. These warriors combine personal combat ability with group support, buffing allies and coordinating attacks. Warlords are natural leaders who enhance the effectiveness of entire parties through their presence and abilities.

## Skills

### 1. Battle Command
**Type:** Party Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Issue a commanding order that increases all party members' attack power by 20% and critical strike chance by 15% for 20 seconds. Your leadership inspires greater performance.

### 2. Tactical Strike
**Type:** Damage / Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Execute a perfectly timed strike that deals 180% weapon damage and applies a vulnerability debuff, causing the target to take 15% more damage from all sources for 10 seconds.

### 3. Rallying Charge
**Type:** Mobility / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Lead a charge that dashes forward, dealing damage to enemies in your path. All allies following you gain 30% movement speed and 10% damage bonus for 8 seconds.

### 4. Inspiring Presence
**Type:** Aura / Passive Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Toggle)  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Activate an inspiring aura that grants all nearby party members 5% increased stats (attack, defense, speed) while active. Costs mana to maintain but provides constant benefit.

### 5. Last Resort
**Type:** Ultimate / Party Save  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** In a desperate moment, unleash your full tactical potential. Restores 50% health and mana to all party members, removes all debuffs, and grants immunity to crowd control for 5 seconds. Ultimate support ability.
