# Warrior

**Role:** Pure physical combatant.  
**Strengths:** High health, simple rotations, frontline presence.

**Differentiation:**  
- Baseline melee DPS or tank
- Minimal mechanics
- Easy to learn

## Description

The Warrior is the foundation of physical combat, representing a master of melee warfare and close-quarters combat. Warriors excel in durability and straightforward damage dealing, making them ideal for players who prefer direct confrontation. With high health pools and powerful physical attacks, Warriors serve as the backbone of any adventuring party, capable of holding the line or delivering devastating blows.

## Skills

### 1. Power Strike
**Type:** Basic Attack  
**Mana Cost:** 10  
**Cooldown:** 3 seconds  
**Description:** A powerful melee strike that deals 150% weapon damage. Basic but effective, this skill forms the core of a Warrior's rotation.

### 2. Shield Bash
**Type:** Interrupt / Stun  
**Mana Cost:** 15  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Bash the target with your shield, dealing moderate damage and stunning them for 2 seconds. Interrupts channeled abilities.

### 3. Battle Cry
**Type:** Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a rallying cry that increases attack power by 15% and generates threat for 10 seconds. Increases party morale.

### 4. Charge
**Type:** Mobility / Gap Closer  
**Mana Cost:** 15  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Charge at an enemy target, closing distance quickly and dealing 120% weapon damage on impact. Stuns the target for 1 second.

### 5. Defensive Stance
**Type:** Defensive Toggle  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Toggle)  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Enter a defensive posture, reducing damage taken by 20% while increasing threat generation. Movement speed reduced by 10% while active.
