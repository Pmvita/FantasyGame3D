# Thief

**Role:** Agile Melee DPS / Utility  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Thieves are nimble combatants who rely on speed, stealth, and cunning rather than brute force. These agile fighters excel at dealing damage from behind enemies and providing utility through crowd control and mobility. Thieves are masters of positioning, using stealth and movement abilities to control engagements.

## Skills

### 1. Stealth
**Type:** Stealth / Mobility  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Enter stealth mode, becoming invisible to enemies for up to 30 seconds or until you attack. Movement speed reduced by 30% while stealthed. Breaking stealth with an attack increases damage by 30%.

### 2. Backstab
**Type:** High Damage (Positional)  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Strike the enemy from behind, dealing 250% weapon damage. Only usable when behind the target. Critical hit chance increased to 100% when used from stealth.

### 3. Evasion
**Type:** Defensive Cooldown  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of heightened awareness for 10 seconds, dodging 75% of all physical attacks. Does not work against magical attacks.

### 4. Vanish
**Type:** Escape / Stealth  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Instantly enter stealth and remove all movement-impairing effects. Grants immunity to damage for 1 second after activation. Emergency escape tool.

### 5. Pick Pocket
**Type:** Utility / Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 15  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Disarm an enemy, removing their weapon for 5 seconds and reducing their damage by 40%. Can also be used to steal items from NPCs (non-combat).
