# Paladin

**Role:** Hybrid Tank/Healer/Support  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Paladins are holy warriors who combine martial prowess with divine magic. These versatile fighters can serve as tanks, healers, or support DPS depending on the situation. With access to both heavy armor and healing spells, Paladins are incredibly durable and provide valuable support to any party composition.

## Skills

### 1. Holy Light
**Type:** Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 3 seconds  
**Description:** Channel holy light to restore health to a friendly target. Can be cast on self or allies. Heals for moderate amount over 2 seconds.

### 2. Consecration
**Type:** Area Damage / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Bless the ground around you, dealing holy damage to enemies standing in the area for 10 seconds. Allies in the area gain 5% damage reduction.

### 3. Lay on Hands
**Type:** Large Heal / Cooldown  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Uses all mana)  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds (5 minutes)  
**Description:** Instantly restore a target to full health. Uses all remaining mana but can save a life in critical moments. Can only be used on yourself or one ally.

### 4. Hammer of Justice
**Type:** Stun / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Throw a divine hammer that stuns the target for 4 seconds and deals moderate holy damage. Effective crowd control for dangerous enemies.

### 5. Divine Protection
**Type:** Defensive Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Grant yourself or an ally divine protection, reducing all damage taken by 40% for 6 seconds. Cannot be dispelled.
