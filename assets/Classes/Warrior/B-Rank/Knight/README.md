# Knight

**Role:** Defensive Tank / Heavy Armor Specialist  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Knights are heavily armored warriors who excel at protecting allies and holding the front line. With superior defense and threat generation, Knights serve as the primary tanks in group content. They combine martial prowess with tactical awareness, using their shields and heavy armor to absorb damage that would devastate other classes.

## Skills

### 1. Shield Wall
**Type:** Defensive Cooldown  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Raise your shield, reducing all incoming damage by 50% for 8 seconds. Generates massive threat while active.

### 2. Taunt
**Type:** Threat Generation  
**Mana Cost:** 10  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Force an enemy to attack you for 4 seconds. Instantly generates high threat and interrupts enemy attacks on allies.

### 3. Shield Slam
**Type:** Damage / Threat  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds  
**Description:** Slam your shield into the enemy, dealing moderate damage and generating significant threat. Bonus damage based on shield's defensive stats.

### 4. Guardian's Aura
**Type:** Party Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Emit an aura that reduces damage taken by all nearby party members by 10% for 20 seconds. Your defensive capabilities are shared with allies.

### 5. Provoke
**Type:** Threat Generation / Range  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Challenge an enemy from range, forcing them to attack you and generating high threat. Can be used on enemies attacking your allies.
