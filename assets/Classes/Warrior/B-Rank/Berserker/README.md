# Berserker

**Role:** High-Risk High-Reward DPS  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Berserkers embrace the thrill of battle, trading defense for devastating offense. These ferocious warriors enter a rage-fueled state, dramatically increasing their damage output while becoming more vulnerable. Berserkers excel at burst damage and solo content, making them dangerous opponents but requiring careful resource and health management.

## Skills

### 1. Berserker Rage
**Type:** Self-Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a berserker state for 20 seconds, increasing attack damage by 30% and attack speed by 25%, but reducing defense by 20%. Cannot be removed while active.

### 2. Bloodthirst
**Type:** Damage / Self-Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a furious attack that deals 180% weapon damage and heals you for 50% of damage dealt. Life-steal effect scales with damage output.

### 3. Whirlwind
**Type:** Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Spin rapidly, dealing damage to all nearby enemies for 3 seconds. Deals 100% weapon damage per second to each target. Can move while spinning.

### 4. Execute
**Type:** Execute / High Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds  
**Description:** A devastating finishing move that deals massive damage to enemies below 30% health. Deals 250% weapon damage, increasing to 400% against enemies below 20% health.

### 5. Reckless Assault
**Type:** Damage / Debuff Self  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Launch a series of reckless attacks, dealing 200% weapon damage per hit for the next 5 attacks. During this time, you take 30% more damage but deal 50% more damage.
