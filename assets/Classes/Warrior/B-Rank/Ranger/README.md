# Ranger

**Role:** Ranged Physical DPS / Tracker  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Rangers are skilled marksmen and trackers who excel at ranged combat and wilderness survival. These versatile fighters use bows and tracking skills to hunt enemies from a distance. Rangers combine physical combat prowess with nature-based abilities, making them excellent scouts and damage dealers.

## Skills

### 1. Aimed Shot
**Type:** High Damage / Channel  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds  
**Description:** Take careful aim and fire a precise shot that deals 250% weapon damage. Damage increases the longer you channel (up to 3 seconds). Critical hits have a 100% chance when fully channeled.

### 2. Track Prey
**Type:** Utility / Detection  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Mark an enemy for tracking, revealing their location through walls and increasing damage dealt to them by 15% for 60 seconds. The mark cannot be removed by the target.

### 3. Multi-Shot
**Type:** Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Fire arrows at up to 5 nearby enemies simultaneously, dealing 130% weapon damage to each. Effective for handling multiple targets at range.

### 4. Trap: Freezing Snare
**Type:** Crowd Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Place a trap that freezes enemies who step on it, rooting them for 5 seconds and dealing frost damage. Up to 3 traps can be active. Traps are invisible to enemies.

### 5. Aspect of the Hawk
**Type:** Mobility / Damage Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enter hawk form for 30 seconds, increasing ranged damage by 35% and movement speed by 40%. While active, you can see enemies from greater distance and attacks have increased range.
