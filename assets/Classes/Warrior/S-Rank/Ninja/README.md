# Ninja

**Role:** Agile Stealth Warrior / Versatile DPS  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (Thief path)

## Description

Ninjas are masters of stealth, speed, and martial arts, combining traditional combat with mystical techniques. These agile warriors excel at hit-and-run tactics and crowd control. Ninjas are incredibly versatile, able to adapt to any combat situation through their diverse skill set.

## Skills

### 1. Shadow Clone
**Type:** Utility / Deception  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds  
**Description:** Create shadow clones that mimic your attacks for 15 seconds. Clones deal 30% of your damage and confuse enemies. Up to 3 clones can be active. Damage taken is distributed among clones.

### 2. Shuriken Barrage
**Type:** Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Throw shurikens in a cone, hitting multiple enemies for 150% weapon damage each. Critical hits reduce the cooldown of other ninja abilities by 2 seconds.

### 3. Smoke Bomb
**Type:** Crowd Control / Escape  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create a smoke cloud that blinds enemies for 5 seconds and allows you to instantly enter stealth. Allies within the smoke gain 20% damage reduction.

### 4. Kunai Strike
**Type:** Gap Closer / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Throw a kunai and teleport to its location, dealing damage to enemies along the path. If the kunai hits an enemy, you appear behind them and deal bonus damage.

### 5. Ultimate Technique: Shadow Storm
**Type:** Ultimate Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Become a whirlwind of shadows for 8 seconds, dealing massive damage to all nearby enemies. While active, you are immune to crowd control and can move freely. Each enemy killed extends the duration by 2 seconds.
