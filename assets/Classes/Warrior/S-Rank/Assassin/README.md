# Assassin

**Role:** Stealth Executioner / Burst DPS  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (Thief path)

## Description

Assassins are elite killers who have perfected the art of stealth and precision strikes. These shadow warriors excel at eliminating high-value targets through devastating opening attacks and critical strikes. Assassins are masters of timing and positioning, dealing immense damage to unsuspecting enemies.

## Skills

### 1. Shadow Strike
**Type:** Stealth Opener / High Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Strike from the shadows, dealing 400% weapon damage when used from stealth. Stuns the target for 3 seconds and applies a damage-over-time effect. Must be in stealth to use.

### 2. Death Mark
**Type:** Execute / Mark Target  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Mark an enemy for death, causing all your attacks against them to deal 50% more damage for 30 seconds. If the marked target dies while marked, all your cooldowns are reduced by 20 seconds.

### 3. Poison Blade
**Type:** Damage over Time / Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Coat your weapon with deadly poison for 20 seconds. All attacks apply a stacking poison that deals damage over time and reduces healing received by 20% per stack (up to 3 stacks).

### 4. Vanish and Ambush
**Type:** Stealth / Gap Closer  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Instantly enter stealth and teleport behind the target, dealing 300% weapon damage. Cannot be used if already in stealth. Powerful engage tool.

### 5. Assassinate
**Type:** Ultimate Execute  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Attempt to instantly kill a target below 40% health, dealing 1000% weapon damage. If the target survives, they are stunned for 5 seconds and take 100% more damage from you for 15 seconds. Only usable from stealth or behind the target.
