# Demon Hunter

**Role:** Anti-Demon Specialist / Hybrid DPS  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Demon Hunters are warriors who have sacrificed everything to fight demonic threats, wielding both weapons and dark magic. These elite fighters excel at hunting and destroying demons, using their own demonic power against their enemies. Demon Hunters are relentless and dangerous, combining martial skill with demonic abilities.

## Skills

### 1. Demon Form
**Type:** Transformation / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Embrace your inner demon for 30 seconds, transforming into a demonic form. While transformed, deal 40% more damage, move 30% faster, and gain access to powerful demonic abilities. Damage taken increases by 20%.

### 2. Chaos Strike
**Type:** Hybrid Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Strike with demonic energy, dealing both physical and dark damage equal to 200% weapon damage. Critical strikes restore 20% mana. Effective against all enemy types.

### 3. Immolation Aura
**Type:** Area Damage / Toggle  
**Mana Cost:** 30 (Initial), 15 per second  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Surround yourself with flames, dealing fire damage to all nearby enemies every second. Costs mana to maintain but provides consistent area damage. Movement speed increased by 10% while active.

### 4. Spectral Sight
**Type:** Utility / Detection  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** See through all illusions and stealth for 20 seconds. Reveals hidden enemies, traps, and demonic entities. Also increases damage dealt to demons by 50% while active.

### 5. Metamorphosis
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Fully transform into a powerful demon for 45 seconds. While transformed, deal 60% more damage, take 30% less damage, and gain powerful wings allowing brief flight. All abilities are enhanced. Ultimate power form.
