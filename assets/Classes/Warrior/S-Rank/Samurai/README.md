# Samurai

**Role:** Master Swordsman / Precision DPS  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Samurais are disciplined warriors who have mastered the way of the sword through years of training and meditation. These honorable fighters excel at precise, powerful strikes and maintaining composure in combat. Samurais combine martial skill with spiritual discipline, making them formidable opponents.

## Skills

### 1. Iaijutsu Strike
**Type:** High Damage / Draw Attack  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Execute a lightning-fast draw strike that deals 350% weapon damage. Damage increases the longer your sword remains sheathed. Can be charged for up to 5 seconds for maximum damage.

### 2. Bushido Stance
**Type:** Buff / Combo System  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Toggle)  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Enter a focused combat stance that increases critical strike chance by 25% and critical strike damage by 50%. Each critical strike builds honor, which can be consumed for powerful finishing moves.

### 3. Zanshin (Awareness)
**Type:** Defensive / Counter  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of heightened awareness for 15 seconds. While active, you automatically parry the next 3 attacks, counter-attacking for 200% weapon damage each time.

### 4. Seppuku
**Type:** Self-Sacrifice / Damage Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Costs 30% health)  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Perform a ritual self-sacrifice, losing 30% of current health to enter a state of ultimate focus for 30 seconds. While active, deal 100% more damage and cannot die (health cannot drop below 1). Must survive to benefit.

### 5. Ultimate Technique: Muramasa's Wrath
**Type:** Ultimate Single Target  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 240 seconds  
**Description:** Channel the spirit of the legendary blade for 3 seconds, then unleash a devastating strike that deals 1000% weapon damage to a single target. Ignores all defenses and always critical hits. The strike can be split among multiple targets if they are close together.
