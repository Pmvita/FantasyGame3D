# Holy Knight

**Role:** Divine Tank / Protector  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (Paladin/Knight path)

## Description

Holy Knights are divine warriors who have achieved perfect harmony between martial skill and holy power. These sacred defenders excel at protecting allies through divine intervention and powerful defensive abilities. Holy Knights are the ultimate protectors, using divine magic to shield their allies from harm.

## Skills

### 1. Divine Shield
**Type:** Ultimate Defense  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds (5 minutes)  
**Description:** Become invulnerable to all damage for 12 seconds. Cannot attack while active but can still protect allies. Emergency survival tool that can save you from certain death.

### 2. Consecrated Ground
**Type:** Area Buff / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Bless the ground around you, creating an area that heals allies, damages undead/demons, and grants 30% damage reduction to all allies within for 30 seconds. The area follows you.

### 3. Avenging Wrath
**Type:** Damage Buff / Threat  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of divine fury for 30 seconds, increasing all damage by 50% and generating massive threat. All attacks have a chance to heal nearby allies for 10% of damage dealt.

### 4. Lay on Hands (Enhanced)
**Type:** Massive Heal / Cooldown  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Uses all mana)  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Instantly restore a target (including yourself) to full health and remove all debuffs. Uses all remaining mana but can save anyone from death. Enhanced version also grants immunity to damage for 3 seconds after healing.

### 5. Guardian Angel
**Type:** Ultimate Protection  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a guardian angel that protects you and all nearby allies for 45 seconds. The angel absorbs 80% of all damage intended for protected targets and provides continuous healing. Cannot be destroyed.
