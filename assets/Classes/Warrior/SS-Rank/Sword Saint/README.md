# Sword Saint

**Role:** Legendary Swordsman / Transcendent Warrior  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior (Samurai/Sword Master path)

## Description

Sword Saints are legendary warriors who have transcended mortal limits, achieving perfect unity of body, mind, and blade. These transcendent fighters have become one with the sword itself, wielding it with divine precision. Sword Saints represent the absolute peak of swordsmanship, capable of cutting through reality itself.

## Skills

### 1. Void Slash
**Type:** Range Attack / Reality Cut  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Slash through reality itself, creating a void that cuts through space. Deals 400% weapon damage to all enemies in a line up to 50 meters. Ignores all physical defenses.

### 2. Perfect Form
**Type:** Buff / Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect form for 45 seconds, increasing all damage by 100%, critical strike chance by 75%, and movement speed by 50%. All abilities have reduced cooldowns while active.

### 3. Counter of All
**Type:** Universal Counter  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of absolute awareness for 10 seconds. Any attack against you is automatically countered for 500% weapon damage. Works against all attack types (physical, magical, ranged).

### 4. Blade Storm
**Type:** Ultimate Area Attack  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Become a storm of blades for 15 seconds, continuously attacking all nearby enemies. Each enemy is hit 20 times, dealing 200% weapon damage per hit. You are immune to all damage while active.

### 5. Cutting Fate
**Type:** Ultimate Reality Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Cut through the very threads of fate itself, dealing 5000% weapon damage to a single target. If the target survives, they are marked for death and take 200% more damage from all sources for 60 seconds. Can only be used once per target per day.
