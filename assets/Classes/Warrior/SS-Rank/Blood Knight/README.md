# Blood Knight

**Role:** Life Steal Tank / DPS Hybrid  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Blood Knights are warriors who have mastered the art of self-sustaining combat through life-stealing techniques. These aggressive tanks excel at maintaining themselves through constant damage dealing, creating a unique playstyle where offense is the best defense. Blood Knights thrive in sustained combat, becoming stronger as they fight longer through their life-steal mechanics.

## Skills

### 1. Blood Rage
**Type:** Self-Buff / Life Steal Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of blood rage for 45 seconds, increasing all life steal by 200% and attack damage by 40%. While in rage, every attack heals you for a massive amount, making you nearly unkillable in prolonged combat. The lower your health, the more life steal you gain.

### 2. Sanguine Strike
**Type:** Damage / Life Steal  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Strike with blood magic, dealing 250% weapon damage and healing yourself for 150% of damage dealt. The life steal cannot be reduced or prevented. Essential for maintaining health through aggression.

### 3. Blood Pool
**Type:** Resource / Health Bank  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Create a pool of stored blood from excess life steal. The pool can hold up to 200% of your maximum health. You can consume the pool to instantly heal or use it to power blood magic abilities. Stores life steal beyond your maximum health.

### 4. Hemorrhage
**Type:** Damage over Time / Life Steal  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Cause the target to bleed profusely, dealing damage every second for 20 seconds. Each tick of damage heals you for 100% of damage dealt. The bleeding cannot be stopped by normal means and stacks up to 3 times.

### 5. Blood Form
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 240 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a being of pure blood for 60 seconds. While transformed, you gain 100% life steal on all attacks, cannot die as long as you're dealing damage (health cannot drop below 1 if enemies are nearby), and all damage dealt heals nearby allies for 25%. Ultimate self-sustain through aggression.
