# Sword Master

**Role:** Ultimate Melee DPS / Precision Fighter  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Sword Masters are warriors who have achieved absolute mastery of the blade, transcending normal combat techniques. These legendary fighters excel at dealing massive damage through perfect technique and timing. Sword Masters represent the pinnacle of melee combat, capable of defeating any opponent through skill alone.

## Skills

### 1. Flurry of Blades
**Type:** Rapid Attacks  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a flurry of perfect strikes, attacking 10 times in 3 seconds. Each strike deals 150% weapon damage and has a 50% chance to critical hit. Can move while attacking.

### 2. Perfect Parry
**Type:** Defensive / Counter  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a perfect defensive stance for 5 seconds. While active, the next 5 attacks against you are automatically parried and countered for 300% weapon damage. Cannot be bypassed.

### 3. Blade Dance
**Type:** Area Damage / Mobility  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Perform a deadly dance with your blade, dealing damage to all nearby enemies while moving freely for 8 seconds. Each enemy hit reduces the cooldown of other abilities by 1 second.

### 4. Critical Mastery
**Type:** Passive Enhancement / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of perfect focus for 30 seconds, increasing critical strike chance to 100% and critical strike damage by 150%. All attacks become critical hits during this time.

### 5. Ultimate Technique: Sever
**Type:** Ultimate Execute  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 240 seconds  
**Description:** Execute the ultimate sword technique, dealing 2000% weapon damage to a single target. If the target is below 50% health, instantly kill them. Ignores all defenses and cannot be blocked or dodged.
