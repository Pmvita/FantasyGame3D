# Death Knight

**Role:** Undead Tank / Dark Warrior  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Warrior

## Description

Death Knights are fallen warriors who have returned from death, wielding dark magic alongside their martial prowess. These undead champions excel at tanking through self-healing and damage mitigation. Death Knights are unstoppable forces who draw power from death itself, making them incredibly difficult to kill.

## Skills

### 1. Death Grip
**Type:** Pull / Threat  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Pull an enemy to you from up to 30 meters away and force them to attack you. Generates massive threat and interrupts channeled abilities. Cannot be resisted.

### 2. Anti-Magic Shell
**Type:** Defensive / Magic Immunity  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 75 seconds  
**Description:** Create a shell of anti-magic energy for 5 seconds that absorbs all magical damage and converts it to health. While active, you are immune to all debuffs and crowd control effects.

### 3. Death Strike
**Type:** Damage / Self-Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds  
**Description:** Strike with dark energy, dealing 200% weapon damage plus dark damage. Heals you for 50% of damage dealt. The lower your health, the more damage and healing this ability provides.

### 4. Army of the Dead
**Type:** Pet Summon  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Raise fallen enemies as undead minions that fight for you for 60 seconds. Minions taunt enemies and deal dark damage. Each enemy killed extends the duration. Can have up to 8 minions.

### 5. Lich King's Blessing
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Embrace the power of the Lich King for 60 seconds, becoming an undead lord. While active, you deal 75% more damage, take 50% less damage, and all your attacks heal you. You cannot die while transformed (health cannot drop below 1).
