# Druid

**Role:** Nature Healer / Shape-shifter  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Druids are healers who have bonded with nature, using the power of the wilds to heal and protect. These nature-connected healers excel at healing over time and shape-shifting into animal forms. Druids are versatile healers who can adapt to any situation through their connection to nature.

## Skills

### 1. Rejuvenation
**Type:** Healing over Time  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Restore health to an ally over 30 seconds. The healing increases over time, with the strongest healing at the end. Can be stacked up to 3 times on the same target. Very mana efficient.

### 2. Bear Form
**Type:** Transformation / Tank  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a bear, increasing health by 200%, armor by 150%, and threat generation by 100%. While in bear form, you can tank effectively but cannot cast healing spells. Great for survival.

### 3. Cat Form
**Type:** Transformation / DPS  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a cat, increasing damage by 100%, movement speed by 50%, and critical strike chance by 30%. While in cat form, you deal significant damage but cannot cast healing spells. Great for solo content.

### 4. Tranquility
**Type:** Area Heal / Channel  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Channel nature's power for 10 seconds, continuously healing all party members within 30 meters. The healing increases over time. Cannot be interrupted. Ultimate group heal.

### 5. Nature's Wrath
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Become one with nature for 60 seconds, gaining access to all animal forms simultaneously. While transformed, you can heal, tank, and deal damage at the same time. All abilities are enhanced. Ultimate druidic power.
