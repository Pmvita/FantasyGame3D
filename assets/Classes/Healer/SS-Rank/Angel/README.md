# Angel

**Role:** Divine Healer / Celestial Support  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer (Divine Priest path)

## Description

Angels are healers who have achieved divine ascension, becoming celestial beings of pure healing energy. These divine healers excel at protecting and restoring allies through pure holy power. Angels can fly and wield divine magic, making them the ultimate protectors.

## Skills

### 1. Divine Healing
**Type:** Massive Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Channel pure divine energy, restoring massive health to a target over 3 seconds. The healing is so powerful it can bring allies back from near-death. Grants a protective shield after healing.

### 2. Wings of Protection
**Type:** Flight / Area Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Spread your wings and take flight for 60 seconds. While flying, you continuously heal all nearby allies and can move freely in three dimensions. Enemies cannot easily reach you.

### 3. Resurrection
**Type:** Revive Ally  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Resurrect a fallen ally with 100% health and mana. Can be cast during combat. The resurrected ally is immune to damage for 5 seconds after revival. Ultimate life-saving ability.

### 4. Celestial Barrier
**Type:** Area Protection  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Create a barrier of divine light that protects all allies within for 45 seconds. The barrier reduces all damage by 50% and reflects 30% back to attackers. Undead and demons take massive damage when touching the barrier.

### 5. Divine Intervention
**Type:** Ultimate Protection  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Call upon divine power to make an ally completely invulnerable for 15 seconds. The protected ally cannot take damage, be debuffed, or die. Can be used on yourself or any ally. Ultimate save ability.
