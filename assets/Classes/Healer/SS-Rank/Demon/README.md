# Demon

**Role:** Dark Healer / Corrupt Support  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer (Necromancer path)

## Description

Demons are healers who have fully embraced dark magic, using corruption and life drain to support allies. These dark healers excel at healing through damage and providing powerful dark buffs. Demons are powerful but risky, using life force and dark magic to heal.

## Skills

### 1. Life Transfer
**Type:** Heal / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Drain life from enemies to heal allies. Deals dark damage to enemies while healing allies for 100% of damage dealt. Can target multiple enemies to heal multiple allies simultaneously.

### 2. Demonic Pact
**Type:** Buff / Resource Exchange  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Form a pact with an ally for 60 seconds, sharing damage and healing. All damage you take is split 50/50 with the linked ally, and all healing is shared. The pact amplifies both partners' power.

### 3. Infernal Healing
**Type:** Area Heal / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create an area of dark energy that heals all allies within while dealing dark damage to all enemies. The area persists for 30 seconds, providing continuous healing and damage. Healing is stronger than damage.

### 4. Possession
**Type:** Control / Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Possess an ally for 30 seconds, taking direct control of their abilities. While possessing, you can heal them directly and enhance all their abilities. The possessed ally gains 50% increased stats.

### 5. True Demon Form
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into your true demonic form for 60 seconds. While transformed, all healing spells also deal dark damage, you gain 75% lifesteal, and can fly. All allies gain dark protection while you're transformed. Ultimate dark power.
