# Monk

**Role:** Martial Healer / Melee Support  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Monks are healers who combine martial arts with healing magic, using physical techniques to restore health and chi energy to heal. These martial healers excel at melee combat while providing support through healing and buffs. Monks are unique healers who fight alongside their allies rather than staying in the back.

## Skills

### 1. Chi Wave
**Type:** Heal / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Send a wave of chi energy that heals allies and damages enemies. The wave bounces between up to 5 targets, prioritizing allies for healing. Each bounce increases the healing/damage by 20%.

### 2. Fists of Fury
**Type:** Rapid Attacks / Combo  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a flurry of punches, attacking 8 times in 2 seconds. Each hit deals damage and has a chance to generate chi. Chi can be consumed for powerful healing abilities. Builds combo points.

### 3. Revival
**Type:** Revival / Emergency Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Revive a fallen ally with 50% health, or if the ally is alive but below 20% health, instantly restore them to 50% health. Can be cast during combat. Emergency life-saving ability.

### 4. Soothing Mist
**Type:** Channeled Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 20 per second  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Channel healing mist at a target ally, continuously restoring health. Can be channeled while moving. Each second of channeling increases the healing amount. Very flexible healing.

### 5. Transcendence
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect harmony between body and spirit for 60 seconds. While transformed, all attacks heal nearby allies, all healing spells deal damage to nearby enemies, and you gain 50% damage reduction. Ultimate martial healer form.
