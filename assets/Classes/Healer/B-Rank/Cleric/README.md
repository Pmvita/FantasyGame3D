# Cleric

**Role:** Traditional Healer / Support  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Clerics are devoted servants of the divine, specializing in healing and protective magic. These healers excel at keeping parties alive through sustained healing and defensive buffs. Clerics have the most straightforward healing toolkit, making them reliable and essential for any group content.

## Skills

### 1. Greater Heal
**Type:** Large Single Target Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 3 seconds  
**Description:** Channel powerful healing energy for 3 seconds, restoring a large amount of health to a friendly target. Most efficient healing spell, best used outside of emergencies.

### 2. Prayer of Mending
**Type:** Healing over Time / Bouncing  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Place a healing prayer on an ally that heals them when they take damage. After healing, the prayer jumps to another injured ally. Can bounce up to 5 times.

### 3. Dispel Magic
**Type:** Debuff Removal / Enemy Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Remove harmful effects from an ally OR beneficial effects from an enemy. More versatile than basic curse removal, works on all magical effects.

### 4. Divine Ward
**Type:** Protective Barrier  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Place a divine ward on an ally that absorbs up to 800 damage. The ward also increases healing received by 20% while active. Lasts 15 seconds or until broken.

### 5. Mass Heal
**Type:** Area Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Instantly restore a moderate amount of health to all party members within 15 meters. Essential for recovering from area-of-effect damage in group content.
