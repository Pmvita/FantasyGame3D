# Bard

**Role:** Musical Support / Buffer  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Bards are healers who use music and song to support allies and hinder enemies. These musical performers excel at providing party-wide buffs and moderate healing through their performances. Bards are versatile support characters who can enhance entire parties through their songs and music magic.

## Skills

### 1. Song of Healing
**Type:** Healing over Time / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 30 per second  
**Cooldown:** None (Toggle)  
**Description:** Play a healing song that continuously restores health to all nearby allies. Can be played while moving and casting other abilities. The song must be maintained with mana per second.

### 2. Song of Power
**Type:** Damage Buff / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 40 per second  
**Cooldown:** None (Toggle)  
**Description:** Play an inspiring song that increases all nearby allies' damage by 20% and critical strike chance by 15%. Can switch between different songs. Multiple bards' songs stack.

### 3. Song of Swiftness
**Type:** Speed Buff / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 35 per second  
**Cooldown:** None (Toggle)  
**Description:** Play a fast-paced song that increases movement speed by 35% and attack speed by 25% for all nearby allies. Essential for mobility and DPS optimization.

### 4. Lullaby
**Type:** Crowd Control / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Play a soothing lullaby that puts all enemies in a large area to sleep for 15 seconds. Sleeping enemies cannot act and take 150% more damage. The sleep breaks on damage but enemies are dazed for 5 seconds after waking.

### 5. Epic Ballad
**Type:** Ultimate Buff / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Perform an epic ballad that grants all nearby allies 30% increased stats, 25% damage reduction, and continuous health and mana regeneration for 60 seconds. The ballad inspires allies to fight at their absolute best. Ultimate musical support.
