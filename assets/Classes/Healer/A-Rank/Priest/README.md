# Priest

**Role:** Advanced Healer / Holy Support  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer (Cleric path)

## Description

Priests are advanced healers who have deepened their connection to the divine, gaining access to more powerful healing magic and holy abilities. These devoted servants excel at both single-target and group healing, with additional utility for dealing with undead and demonic enemies. Priests are versatile healers capable of adapting to any situation.

## Skills

### 1. Divine Intervention
**Type:** Massive Heal / Cooldown  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Channel divine power for 3 seconds, restoring a massive amount of health to a target ally. Can save even the most critically wounded. Grants a protective buff for 10 seconds after healing.

### 2. Holy Word: Sanctify
**Type:** Area Heal / Cleanse  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Create a holy area that continuously heals all allies within it and removes debuffs every 2 seconds. The area lasts 8 seconds. Very powerful for group healing scenarios.

### 3. Smite
**Type:** Damage (vs Undead/Demons)  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 3 seconds  
**Description:** Deal holy damage to an enemy target. Deals 200% increased damage to undead and demonic enemies. Provides moderate DPS capability for healers.

### 4. Power Word: Shield
**Type:** Protective Barrier  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Instantly place a powerful shield on an ally that absorbs damage equal to 50% of their maximum health. The shield also prevents spell interruption while active. Lasts 15 seconds or until broken.

### 5. Angelic Form
**Type:** Transformation / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into an angelic being for 20 seconds. While transformed, all healing spells cost 50% less mana, heal for 50% more, and you gain the ability to hover/fly. Ultimate healing form.
