# Divine Priest

**Role:** Master Healer / Divine Support  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer (Priest path)

## Description

Divine Priests are the ultimate healers, having achieved perfect communion with the divine. These legendary healers can restore life itself and protect entire armies with their prayers. Divine Priests excel at both emergency healing and sustained group support, making them essential for the most challenging content.

## Skills

### 1. Divine Intervention
**Type:** Ultimate Heal / Revival  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Call upon divine power to instantly restore a target to full health and remove all debuffs. If the target is dead, resurrect them with 75% health. Can save anyone from certain death once per cooldown.

### 2. Prayer Circle
**Type:** Area Heal / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Create a sacred circle that continuously heals all allies within for 30 seconds. Allies in the circle also gain 25% increased damage and 20% damage reduction. The circle follows you.

### 3. Holy Word: Salvation
**Type:** Mass Heal / Cleanse  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Channel divine energy for 4 seconds, healing all party members for a massive amount and removing all debuffs. Cannot be interrupted while channeling. Ultimate group save.

### 4. Guardian Angel
**Type:** Protective Summon  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a guardian angel that protects a target ally for 60 seconds. The angel absorbs all damage intended for the target and provides continuous healing. If the target would die, the angel sacrifices itself to prevent death once.

### 5. Ascension
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Ascend to a divine form for 45 seconds, becoming a being of pure healing energy. While transformed, all healing spells cost no mana, heal for 200% more, and have no cooldowns. You can fly and are immune to all damage. Ultimate healing form.
