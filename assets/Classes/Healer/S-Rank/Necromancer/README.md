# Necromancer

**Role:** Dark Healer / Undead Master  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Necromancers are healers who have embraced death magic, using the power of undeath to sustain themselves and their allies. These dark healers can heal through life drain, raise the dead, and command undead minions. Necromancers are unique healers who blur the line between life and death, using dark magic for beneficial purposes.

## Skills

### 1. Life Drain
**Type:** Heal / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Drain life from an enemy to heal yourself or an ally. Deals dark damage to the target while healing for 100% of damage dealt. Can be channeled for up to 6 seconds for massive healing.

### 2. Raise Dead
**Type:** Pet Summon / Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Raise a fallen enemy as an undead minion that fights for you for 60 seconds. The undead minion has the same stats as the original enemy but deals dark damage. Can have up to 3 minions active.

### 3. Death Ward
**Type:** Protective Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Place a death ward on an ally that prevents death for 10 seconds. If they would die, they are instead healed to 50% health and become immune to damage for 3 seconds. Can only be used once per target per cooldown.

### 4. Undead Transformation
**Type:** Self-Buff / Hybrid  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into an undead form for 30 seconds, gaining 50% damage reduction and immunity to death effects. While transformed, all healing spells also deal dark damage to nearby enemies. Can heal through damage.

### 5. Army of the Dead
**Type:** Ultimate Pet Army  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Raise all fallen enemies in a large area as undead minions that fight for you for 90 seconds. Each minion deals significant dark damage. The army grows stronger with each enemy killed. Ultimate necromantic power.
