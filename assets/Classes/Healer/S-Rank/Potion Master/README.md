# Potion Master

**Role:** Alchemical Healer / Support Specialist  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Potion Masters are healers who have mastered the art of alchemy, using potions and elixirs to support allies. These crafty healers excel at providing buffs and healing through consumable items. Potion Masters are versatile support healers who can adapt to any situation through their extensive knowledge of alchemical formulas.

## Skills

### 1. Healing Potion
**Type:** Instant Heal / Consumable  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 3 seconds  
**Description:** Instantly create and throw a healing potion that restores a large amount of health to the target. The potion can be thrown to allies or used on yourself. Can hold up to 5 potions at once.

### 2. Elixir of Power
**Type:** Buff Potion  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create an elixir that increases the target's damage by 30% and critical strike chance by 20% for 30 minutes. Can be given to multiple allies but costs resources. Lasts through death.

### 3. Poison Antidote
**Type:** Cure / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Create an antidote that removes all poisons and curses from the target and grants immunity to them for 15 seconds. Essential for dealing with debuff-heavy encounters.

### 4. Regeneration Potion
**Type:** Healing over Time  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Create a potion that restores health and mana over 30 seconds. The regeneration continues even during combat and stacks with other regeneration effects. Can be used on multiple targets.

### 5. Master Elixir
**Type:** Ultimate Buff Potion  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Create the ultimate elixir that grants massive stat increases (50% to all stats), immunity to crowd control, and continuous regeneration for 60 seconds. Can be given to one ally or used on yourself. Ultimate support consumable.
