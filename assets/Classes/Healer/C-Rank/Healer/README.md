# Healer

**Role:** Support and restoration specialist.  
**Strengths:** Healing abilities, buffs, group utility, defensive support.

**Differentiation:**  
- Restores health and removes debuffs from allies
- Essential for group content and challenging encounters
- Reactive playstyle with resource management focus
- Lower damage output compared to DPS classes, but high group value

## Description

The Healer is a guardian of life, dedicated to preserving and protecting allies through powerful restoration magic. Healers are essential in any group composition, keeping party members alive through dangerous encounters. With a focus on reactive gameplay and resource management, Healers must carefully balance their mana reserves while prioritizing the most critical heals. Though they deal minimal damage, their presence often determines the difference between victory and defeat.

## Skills

### 1. Heal
**Type:** Single Target Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 2 seconds  
**Description:** Restore health to a friendly target over 2.5 seconds. Moderate healing amount, best used for sustained healing.

### 2. Flash Heal
**Type:** Instant Single Target Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 1 second  
**Description:** Instantly restore a large amount of health to a friendly target. High mana cost but crucial for emergency healing.

### 3. Remove Curse
**Type:** Debuff Removal  
**Mana Cost:** 15  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Remove one harmful magical effect from a friendly target. Essential for handling enemy debuffs and curses.

### 4. Shield of Light
**Type:** Protective Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Place a protective barrier on an ally that absorbs the next 500 damage. Lasts 10 seconds if not broken.

### 5. Prayer of Healing
**Type:** Area Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Channel healing energy for 3 seconds, restoring health to all nearby allies every second. Effective for group healing situations.
