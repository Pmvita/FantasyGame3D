# Summoner

**Role:** Ultimate Pet Master / Minion Commander  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Summoners are healers who have mastered the art of summoning, commanding powerful beings to fight and heal alongside them. These ultimate pet masters excel at supporting through summoned allies rather than direct healing. Summoners can summon entire armies and command them with perfect control, making them incredibly versatile.

## Skills

### 1. Summon Army
**Type:** Mass Pet Summon  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Summon an army of 20 various minions to fight for you. The army includes healers, tanks, and damage dealers. All minions are under your perfect control and share your stats. The army persists for 180 seconds or until all minions are destroyed.

### 2. Master Summon
**Type:** Powerful Pet Summon  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a powerful being (dragon, angel, demon, elemental lord) to fight for you for 120 seconds. The summoned being is incredibly powerful and can single-handedly turn the tide of battle. Different summons available based on situation.

### 3. Healing Minions
**Type:** Summoned Healers  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Summon specialized healing minions that continuously heal all nearby allies for 120 seconds. The minions prioritize critical health allies and can resurrect fallen allies. Up to 10 healing minions can be active.

### 4. Minion Mastery
**Type:** Pet Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Enhance all your active minions for 180 seconds, increasing their stats by 200% and granting them new abilities. Enhanced minions become incredibly powerful and can rival player characters in effectiveness.

### 5. Ultimate Summon: Divine Host
**Type:** Ultimate Pet Army  
**Mana Cost:** 300  
**Cooldown:** 900 seconds (15 minutes)  
**Description:** Summon the ultimate army: 50 elite minions of various types, 5 powerful generals, and 1 legendary commander. The army is perfectly coordinated and incredibly powerful, capable of taking on entire raids. All minions are enhanced and can be commanded with perfect precision. The army persists for 300 seconds. Ultimate summoning power.
