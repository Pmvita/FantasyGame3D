# Sage

**Role:** Ultimate Wisdom Healer / Knowledge Keeper  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer

## Description

Sages are healers who have achieved perfect wisdom, using knowledge and understanding to heal and protect. These ultimate wisdom keepers have transcended traditional healing, using the power of knowledge itself to restore and enhance. Sages represent the pinnacle of healing through understanding, wielding wisdom as a weapon and a shield.

## Skills

### 1. Wisdom of Ages
**Type:** Universal Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Share the wisdom of ages with all allies, increasing all their stats by 30% and making them immune to all negative effects for 60 seconds. The wisdom also increases experience gain and learning speed.

### 2. Perfect Understanding
**Type:** Information / Advantage  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect understanding of all things for 120 seconds. You know all enemy abilities, weaknesses, and strategies. All healing is 100% more effective, and you can prevent all incoming damage by understanding attack patterns.

### 3. Knowledge Transfer
**Type:** Ability Sharing  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Transfer your knowledge to allies, allowing them to use any of your abilities for 60 seconds. Allies gain access to your entire spellbook and can use any healing or support ability you know. Ultimate knowledge sharing.

### 4. Eternal Wisdom
**Type:** Continuous Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Bestow eternal wisdom upon an ally for 180 seconds. The ally gains massive stat increases, learns all your abilities temporarily, and becomes nearly invulnerable through perfect understanding of all threats. Can only affect one ally at a time.

### 5. Absolute Wisdom
**Type:** Ultimate Enlightenment  
**Mana Cost:** 250  
**Cooldown:** 900 seconds (15 minutes)  
**Description:** Achieve absolute wisdom, becoming omniscient for 180 seconds. While in this state, you know everything: all past, present, and future events; all possible outcomes; all knowledge. You can prevent any damage, heal any wound, and solve any problem through perfect understanding. You become one with knowledge itself. Ultimate wisdom transcendence.
