# Arch Demon

**Role:** Ultimate Dark Healer / Infernal Being  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer (Demon path)

## Description

Arch Demons are healers who have fully embraced dark power, becoming true infernal beings of corruption and life force manipulation. These ultimate dark healers use the power of the void itself to heal and protect, turning life and death into weapons. Arch Demons represent the absolute peak of dark healing, wielding the power of the underworld.

## Skills

### 1. Soul Harvest
**Type:** Damage / Mass Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Harvest souls from all enemies in a large area, dealing massive dark damage and healing all allies for 150% of total damage dealt. The more enemies, the more healing. Ultimate area heal through damage.

### 2. Infernal Form
**Type:** Transformation / Flight  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into your true demonic form for 120 seconds, gaining massive demonic wings and the ability to fly. While transformed, all healing spells also deal dark damage, you gain 100% lifesteal, and you can raise fallen enemies as undead servants.

### 3. Death Pact
**Type:** Ultimate Resource Exchange  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Form a pact with an ally that prevents their death for 60 seconds. If they would die, they are instead healed to full health and you take the damage instead. The pact can be broken early to transfer all damage to enemies.

### 4. Void Healing
**Type:** Reverse Healing  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Use the void itself to heal, restoring health by drawing from the void. The healing is instant and massive, but deals void damage to nearby enemies equal to 50% of healing done. Can heal through death itself.

### 5. Infernal Ascension
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 250  
**Cooldown:** 900 seconds (15 minutes)  
**Description:** Achieve true demonic ascension, becoming a being of pure infernal energy for 180 seconds. While transformed, you are a demon lord: all healing becomes damage to enemies, all damage becomes healing to allies, and you can control life and death itself. You become immortal and can resurrect unlimited times. Ultimate dark transcendence.
