# Arch Angel

**Role:** Ultimate Divine Healer / Celestial Being  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Healer (Angel path)

## Description

Arch Angels are healers who have achieved perfect divine ascension, becoming true celestial beings of pure healing energy. These ultimate divine healers can restore life itself and protect entire armies through their divine connection. Arch Angels represent the absolute pinnacle of healing, wielding the power of creation itself.

## Skills

### 1. Divine Restoration
**Type:** Ultimate Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Channel pure divine energy to instantly restore any target to full health and remove all debuffs. The healing is so powerful it can restore lost limbs and reverse death if cast quickly enough. Grants divine protection for 30 seconds after healing.

### 2. Celestial Form
**Type:** Flight / Enhanced Abilities  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into your true celestial form for 120 seconds, gaining massive wings and the ability to fly. While transformed, all healing spells are instant-cast, cost 50% less mana, and heal for 200% more. You are immune to all damage.

### 3. Mass Resurrection
**Type:** Group Revival  
**Mana Cost:** 200  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Resurrect all fallen allies within 100 meters with 100% health and mana. Can be cast during combat. The resurrected allies are immune to damage for 10 seconds. Ultimate group save ability.

### 4. Divine Aegis
**Type:** Ultimate Protection  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Encase all allies in a divine barrier that makes them completely invulnerable to all damage for 30 seconds. The barrier cannot be broken and protects against all attack types. Undead and demons take massive damage when attacking protected targets.

### 5. Apotheosis: Divine Ascension
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 250  
**Cooldown:** 900 seconds (15 minutes)  
**Description:** Achieve true divine ascension, becoming a being of pure divine energy for 180 seconds. While transformed, you are a god of healing: all healing spells have no cost or cooldown, you can resurrect unlimited times, and you can prevent death itself. All allies within 100 meters are continuously healed to full health. Ultimate healing transcendence.
