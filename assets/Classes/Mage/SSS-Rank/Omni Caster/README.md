# Omni Caster

**Role:** Master of All Elements / Universal Mage  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Omni Casters are mages who have achieved perfect mastery over all elements simultaneously, transcending the limitations of elemental specialization. These ultimate casters can wield fire, water, wind, earth, light, dark, and arcane magic with equal perfection. Omni Casters represent the pinnacle of elemental magic, capable of creating devastating combinations of all elements.

## Skills

### 1. Elemental Fusion
**Type:** Combined Element Attack  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Combine all elements into a single devastating attack. The fusion deals damage of all types simultaneously, making it impossible to resist. Each element amplifies the others, resulting in exponentially increased damage.

### 2. Omni Shield
**Type:** Universal Defense  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Create a shield that adapts to resist any damage type. The shield automatically adjusts its resistance based on incoming attacks, providing 80% resistance to all damage types simultaneously. Lasts 60 seconds.

### 3. Elemental Mastery
**Type:** Universal Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect control over all elements for 90 seconds. All elemental damage is increased by 150%, and you can switch between any element instantly. All elemental spells cost 50% less mana and have reduced cooldowns.

### 4. Chaos Storm
**Type:** Area Devastation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Create a storm of all elements simultaneously, dealing catastrophic damage of every type in a huge area for 30 seconds. The storm combines fire, ice, lightning, earth, light, and dark energy, making it impossible to resist completely.

### 5. Elemental Ascension
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 200  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Ascend to become one with all elements for 180 seconds. While transformed, you are a being of pure elemental energy, immune to all elemental damage, able to control weather, and all your spells combine all elements automatically. You become a walking apocalypse. Ultimate elemental power.
