# Reality Architect

**Role:** Terrain Creator / Rule Breaker  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Reality Architects are mages who have transcended normal spellcasting, gaining the ability to create terrain and alter the fundamental rules of combat itself. These ultimate mages break traditional combat logic by reshaping the battlefield to their will. Reality Architects can create walls, platforms, safe zones, and even alter gravity and physics, making them incredibly powerful but requiring immense creativity and strategic thinking.

## Skills

### 1. Terrain Creation
**Type:** Environment Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Create terrain features at will: walls for protection, platforms for positioning, pits to trap enemies, or ramps for movement. The terrain persists for 5 minutes or until destroyed. Can create up to 10 terrain pieces simultaneously. Breaks traditional combat positioning.

### 2. Rule Alteration
**Type:** Combat Logic Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Alter a fundamental rule of combat for 60 seconds. Examples: reverse gravity, make ranged attacks curve, allow allies to walk on walls, or make enemies take damage when moving. Each alteration breaks normal combat logic in unique ways. Requires strategic thinking to use effectively.

### 3. Safe Zone
**Type:** Area Protection / Rule Breaking  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 240 seconds  
**Description:** Create a zone where your rules apply absolutely for 90 seconds. Within the zone, you can set custom rules: no damage taken, infinite mana, instant cooldowns, etc. The zone persists until destroyed or duration ends. Ultimate area control through rule breaking.

### 4. Physics Manipulation
**Type:** Environmental Control  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Manipulate physics in an area for 60 seconds: change gravity direction, alter projectile trajectories, create force fields, or reverse momentum. Enemies caught off-guard by physics changes take massive damage. Creative use creates unique tactical advantages.

### 5. Reality Reconstruction
**Type:** Ultimate Terrain Control  
**Mana Cost:** 200  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Completely reconstruct the battlefield to your design for 180 seconds. Create an entire new battlefield with custom terrain, rules, and physics. You become the master of the arena, controlling every aspect of combat. Enemies must adapt to your reality or be overwhelmed. Ultimate rule-breaking power that transcends normal combat logic entirely.
