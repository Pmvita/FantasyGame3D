# Spell Breaker

**Role:** Anti-Mage Specialist / Magic Negator  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Spell Breakers are mages who have mastered the art of negating and countering magic itself. These anti-magic specialists excel at neutralizing enemy spellcasters and turning their magic against them. Spell Breakers are the ultimate mage hunters, using their knowledge of magic to completely shut down magical threats.

## Skills

### 1. Spell Steal
**Type:** Steal Enemy Buff / Dispel  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Steal a beneficial effect from an enemy and transfer it to yourself, or remove a harmful effect from an ally and transfer it to an enemy. Can steal any magical effect, including transformation buffs. Ultimate utility.

### 2. Magic Immunity
**Type:** Complete Magic Negation  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Become completely immune to all magical damage and effects for 20 seconds. All spells cast at you are absorbed and converted to mana. Enemy spellcasters cannot target you while active. Ultimate magic defense.

### 3. Counterspell Mastery
**Type:** Universal Counter  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Counter any spell, regardless of type or protection. The countered spell is reflected back to the caster for 200% damage. Works even against normally uncounterable spells and ultimate abilities.

### 4. Mana Burn
**Type:** Resource Drain / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Burn an enemy's mana, dealing damage equal to 200% of mana burned. If the target has no mana, deals damage based on their maximum mana instead. Destroys enemy spellcasters' ability to fight.

### 5. Null Field
**Type:** Ultimate Magic Negation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Create a massive field of anti-magic energy that completely negates all magic within it for 60 seconds. Enemy spells cannot be cast, active spells are dispelled, and magical effects are nullified. Your own magic is enhanced within the field. Ultimate anti-magic zone.
