# Akashic Caster

**Role:** Reality Manipulator / Record Keeper  
**Rank:** SSS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Akashic Casters are mages who have tapped into the Akashic Records, the universal library of all knowledge and possibility. These transcendent casters can read and manipulate reality itself through access to all information that ever was or will be. Akashic Casters represent the ultimate evolution of magic, wielding knowledge itself as a weapon.

## Skills

### 1. Read the Records
**Type:** Information / Advantage  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Read the Akashic Records to learn everything about your target: weaknesses, resistances, upcoming attacks, and optimal strategies. All damage against the target is increased by 75% for 60 seconds. You gain perfect knowledge of all enemy abilities.

### 2. Rewrite Reality
**Type:** Reality Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Rewrite a small portion of reality, changing the outcome of events. Can undo damage taken, reverse enemy buffs, or alter enemy attacks. The rewrite persists for 30 seconds. Can prevent one death per cooldown cycle.

### 3. Probability Manipulation
**Type:** Luck Control  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Control probability itself for 60 seconds. All your attacks have 100% critical hit chance, all enemy attacks have 0% critical hit chance against you, and all random effects favor you. Ultimate advantage manipulation.

### 4. Access All Spells
**Type:** Spell Library Access  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Access the Akashic Records to learn and use any spell from any class or school of magic. You can prepare and use any combination of spells, limited only by your mana pool. Ultimate spell versatility.

### 5. Absolute Knowledge
**Type:** Ultimate Enlightenment  
**Mana Cost:** 200  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Achieve perfect knowledge of all things for 120 seconds. While active, you know everything: all enemy positions, all upcoming attacks, all possible outcomes. You can cast any spell instantly with no mana cost, and all abilities have no cooldowns. You become omniscient and omnipotent. Ultimate magical transcendence.
