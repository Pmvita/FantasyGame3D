# Battle Mage

**Role:** Melee Magic Fighter / Hybrid DPS  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Battle Mages blur the line between warrior and mage, wielding magic-enhanced weapons in close combat. These versatile fighters combine melee attacks with instant-cast spells, making them effective in both ranged and melee situations. Battle Mages use magical barriers and enchantments to survive in melee while dealing both physical and magical damage.

## Skills

### 1. Arcane Weapon
**Type:** Buff / Damage Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enchant your weapon with arcane energy for 30 seconds, causing all melee attacks to deal additional arcane damage equal to 30% of weapon damage. Attacks have a chance to restore mana.

### 2. Magic Shield
**Type:** Defensive / Mana Conversion  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Uses Mana as Shield)  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Create a shield that absorbs damage by consuming mana instead of health. Each point of damage absorbed consumes 2 mana. Lasts until mana is depleted or manually cancelled.

### 3. Blade Surge
**Type:** Gap Closer / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Dash forward with magical speed, dealing arcane damage to all enemies in your path. Closes distance quickly and can pass through enemies.

### 4. Spell Strike
**Type:** Melee / Spell Combo  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds  
**Description:** Infuse your weapon attack with magic, dealing 150% weapon damage plus arcane damage. Each successful hit reduces the cooldown of your next spell by 1 second.

### 5. Aegis of Power
**Type:** Defensive / Offensive Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Surround yourself with protective magic for 15 seconds. While active, you take 30% less damage and deal 25% more damage. Damage reduction increases as your health decreases.
