# Enchanter

**Role:** Buff Specialist / Support Mage  
**Rank:** B-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Enchanters are mages who specialize in enhancing allies and weakening enemies through magical enhancements. These support casters excel at providing powerful buffs and debuffs rather than direct damage. Enchanters are highly valued in group content for their ability to significantly increase party effectiveness.

## Skills

### 1. Enchant Weapon
**Type:** Weapon Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds (5 minutes)  
**Description:** Permanently enchant a weapon until death or replacement, increasing its damage by 25% and adding elemental damage. The enchantment persists through combat and cannot be dispelled. Can enchant one weapon at a time.

### 2. Haste
**Type:** Speed Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Grant an ally increased attack speed by 40% and movement speed by 30% for 30 seconds. Can be cast on multiple allies. Essential for DPS optimization.

### 3. Weakness Curse
**Type:** Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Curse an enemy, reducing their damage output by 25% and defense by 20% for 45 seconds. Can be applied to multiple enemies. Stacks with other debuffs.

### 4. Magic Shield
**Type:** Protective Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Grant an ally a magic shield that absorbs damage equal to 40% of their maximum health. The shield also increases magical resistance by 30%. Lasts 60 seconds or until broken.

### 5. Group Enchantment
**Type:** Area Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Enchant all nearby allies for 90 seconds, increasing all their stats by 15% and granting them immunity to minor debuffs. The enchantment cannot be dispelled by normal means. Ultimate group enhancement.
