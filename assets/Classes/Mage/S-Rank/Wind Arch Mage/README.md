# Wind Arch Mage

**Role:** Master Aeromancer / Ultimate Mobility  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Wind Mage path)

## Description

Wind Arch Mages have transcended the boundaries of air magic, becoming one with the wind itself. These legendary aeromancers can control weather and move with impossible speed. Wind Arch Mages excel at rapid repositioning and controlling enemy movement through absolute mastery of atmospheric forces.

## Skills

### 1. Storm Surge
**Type:** Area Damage / Movement Control  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 50 seconds  
**Description:** Create a powerful storm that continuously pushes enemies away from the center while dealing wind damage every second for 15 seconds. Can be used to control enemy positioning.

### 2. Wind Walking
**Type:** Flight / Mobility  
**Mana Cost:** 50 (Initial), 20 per second  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Take flight, allowing you to move in three dimensions for up to 30 seconds. While flying, you can cast spells and are immune to ground-based attacks. Ultimate mobility.

### 3. Cyclone Prison
**Type:** Crowd Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Trap enemies in powerful cyclones that lift them into the air, dealing continuous wind damage for 10 seconds. Trapped enemies cannot act and take 150% more damage from all sources.

### 4. Gale Force
**Type:** Area Buff / Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 75 seconds  
**Description:** Create a gale that increases all allies' movement speed by 50% and attack speed by 25% while slowing all enemies by 50% for 20 seconds. Powerful battlefield control.

### 5. Tempest's Eye
**Type:** Ultimate Area Devastation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a massive hurricane that devastates a huge area for 25 seconds. The storm continuously damages enemies, lifts them into the air, and throws them around. Allies gain immunity to the storm's effects and increased movement speed.
