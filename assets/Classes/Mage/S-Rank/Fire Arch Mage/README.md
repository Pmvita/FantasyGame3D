# Fire Arch Mage

**Role:** Master Pyromancer / Extreme Burst Damage  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Fire Mage path)

## Description

Fire Arch Mages are the ultimate masters of pyromancy, having transcended the limits of fire magic. These legendary casters can incinerate entire armies with their mastery over flame. Fire Arch Mages represent the pinnacle of destructive magical power, capable of unleashing apocalyptic levels of fire damage.

## Skills

### 1. Infernal Rain
**Type:** Area Damage / Continuous  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a rain of fire that lasts 15 seconds, dealing massive fire damage to all enemies in a large area every 2 seconds. The ground becomes scorched, dealing additional damage over time.

### 2. Phoenix Flames
**Type:** Damage / Resurrection Effect  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash phoenix flames that deal extreme fire damage. If you die while this ability is on cooldown, you are resurrected with 50% health once per cooldown cycle. The flames burn enemies for 30 seconds.

### 3. Plasma Beam
**Type:** Channeled Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 60 per second  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Channel a beam of superheated plasma for up to 8 seconds, dealing extreme fire damage per second. The beam penetrates through all enemies and can be moved while channeling.

### 4. Combustion Mastery
**Type:** Passive Enhancement / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Enhance all fire damage by 50% for 30 seconds. All fire damage-over-time effects deal 3x damage and spread to nearby enemies. Ultimate fire power amplifier.

### 5. Apocalypse
**Type:** Ultimate Area Devastation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Call upon the power of a volcanic apocalypse, creating multiple massive fire explosions across a huge area for 12 seconds. Deals catastrophic fire damage and leaves the ground burning for 30 seconds. Ultimate destruction.
