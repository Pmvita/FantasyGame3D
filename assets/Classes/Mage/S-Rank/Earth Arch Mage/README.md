# Earth Arch Mage

**Role:** Master Geomancer / Ultimate Defense  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Earth Mage path)

## Description

Earth Arch Mages have achieved complete mastery over the earth itself, moving mountains and reshaping the battlefield. These legendary geomancers are the tankiest of all mages, using earth magic for both offense and unparalleled defense. Earth Arch Mages control the very ground beneath their feet, making them incredibly difficult to defeat.

## Skills

### 1. Mountain's Aegis
**Type:** Ultimate Defense  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Become one with the mountain for 20 seconds, reducing all damage taken by 70% and becoming immune to crowd control. Cannot be moved or knocked back. Reflects 50% of damage taken back to attackers.

### 2. Continental Shift
**Type:** Area Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 100 seconds  
**Description:** Reshape the battlefield, creating elevated platforms for allies and pits for enemies. Deals massive earth damage and stuns enemies for 5 seconds. The terrain changes persist for 60 seconds.

### 3. Stone Golem Summon
**Type:** Pet Summon  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a massive stone golem that fights for you for 90 seconds. The golem has massive health, taunts enemies, and deals significant earth damage. Can be commanded to protect specific allies.

### 4. Diamond Skin
**Type:** Defensive Buff / Damage Reflect  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Transform your skin to diamond for 30 seconds, reducing all damage by 60% and reflecting 100% of physical damage back to attackers. Magical damage is reduced but not reflected.

### 5. World Breaker
**Type:** Ultimate Single Target Devastation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Channel the power of the planet itself, creating a massive earthquake that focuses on a single target area. Deals catastrophic earth damage and stuns all enemies in a huge radius for 8 seconds. The ground remains unstable for 30 seconds.
