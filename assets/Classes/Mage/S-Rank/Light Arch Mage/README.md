# Light Arch Mage

**Role:** Master Luminomancer / Divine Destruction  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Light Mage path)

## Description

Light Arch Mages have achieved perfect harmony with divine light energy, wielding pure radiance as a weapon. These legendary luminomancers excel at destroying darkness and undead while providing support to allies. Light Arch Mages represent the pinnacle of holy magic, capable of banishing evil with pure light.

## Skills

### 1. Solar Flare
**Type:** Area Damage / Blind  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a blinding flash of solar energy that deals massive light damage to all enemies in a large area and blinds them for 8 seconds. Undead and demons take 300% increased damage.

### 2. Divine Aegis
**Type:** Party Protection  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Surround all party members with divine protection for 30 seconds, reducing all damage taken by 40% and healing them for 5% of maximum health every 3 seconds. Undead and demonic attacks are completely negated.

### 3. Purifying Light
**Type:** Area Cleanse / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Create an area of pure light that removes all debuffs from allies and deals continuous light damage to enemies for 20 seconds. The area grows larger over time.

### 4. Resurrection
**Type:** Revival  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 600 seconds (10 minutes)  
**Description:** Resurrect a fallen ally with 50% health and mana. Can be cast during combat but has a long cast time. Essential for difficult encounters. Only one resurrection per target per encounter.

### 5. Apotheosis
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Ascend to a divine form for 45 seconds, becoming a being of pure light. While transformed, all light damage is increased by 100%, you can fly, and all healing spells are instant cast with no mana cost. Ultimate power form.
