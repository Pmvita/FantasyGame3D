# Dark Arch Mage

**Role:** Master Shadowmancer / Ultimate Corruption  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Dark Mage path)

## Description

Dark Arch Mages have fully embraced the void, wielding shadows and corruption with absolute mastery. These legendary shadowmancers excel at sustained damage through powerful curses and shadow magic. Dark Arch Mages represent the pinnacle of dark magic, capable of corrupting reality itself.

## Skills

### 1. Void Eruption
**Type:** Area Damage / Corruption  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create an eruption of void energy that deals massive dark damage and corrupts the area for 30 seconds. Corrupted ground deals continuous damage and reduces healing received by 50% for enemies standing on it.

### 2. Soul Rend
**Type:** Damage over Time / Life Drain  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Rend the target's soul, dealing extreme dark damage over 30 seconds and healing yourself for 75% of damage dealt. The damage increases over time and cannot be dispelled.

### 3. Shadow Realm
**Type:** Area Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Drag enemies into a shadow realm for 20 seconds, dealing continuous dark damage and preventing them from seeing the normal world. Enemies are slowed by 60% and take 50% more damage from shadow magic.

### 4. Corruption Mastery
**Type:** Debuff Amplification  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 75 seconds  
**Description:** Amplify all dark damage and curses by 100% for 30 seconds. All debuffs deal damage over time, and new debuffs last twice as long. Ultimate dark power amplifier.

### 5. Ultimate Void
**Type:** Ultimate Area Devastation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Open a gateway to the void itself, creating an area of absolute darkness for 25 seconds. All enemies within take catastrophic dark damage, are slowed by 80%, and cannot see beyond the void. Allies are unaffected and gain shadow protection.
