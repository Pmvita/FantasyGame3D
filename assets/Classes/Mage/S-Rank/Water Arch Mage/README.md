# Water Arch Mage

**Role:** Master Hydromancer / Ultimate Control  
**Rank:** S-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Water Mage path)

## Description

Water Arch Mages have achieved complete mastery over water and ice magic, wielding the oceans themselves as weapons. These legendary hydromancers can flood entire battlefields and freeze enemies solid with a thought. Water Arch Mages excel at battlefield control and area denial through their absolute command of liquid and frozen states.

## Skills

### 1. Tidal Mastery
**Type:** Area Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 50 seconds  
**Description:** Command massive tidal forces, creating waves that push and damage enemies while healing allies. The water persists for 20 seconds, continuing to affect the battlefield.

### 2. Absolute Zero
**Type:** Crowd Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Freeze all enemies in a large area solid for 8 seconds. Frozen enemies take 200% more damage and cannot act. The ice shatters after the duration, dealing additional damage.

### 3. Water Clone
**Type:** Utility / Deception  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Create water clones that mirror your movements and cast spells. Clones deal 40% of your damage and explode for area damage when destroyed. Up to 5 clones can be active.

### 4. Healing Spring
**Type:** Area Heal / Resource  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create a healing spring that restores health and mana to all allies standing in it for 30 seconds. The spring also removes debuffs every 5 seconds. Powerful support ability.

### 5. Poseidon's Wrath
**Type:** Ultimate Area Devastation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Summon the fury of the ocean, creating a massive tsunami that floods the battlefield for 20 seconds. All enemies are continuously damaged and slowed by 70%. Allies gain massive healing over time while in the water.
