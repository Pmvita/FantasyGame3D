# Dark Mage

**Role:** Shadow Magic Specialist / Damage over Time  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Dark Mages embrace shadow and dark magic, specializing in damage-over-time effects and debilitating curses. These shadowmancers excel at weakening enemies and dealing sustained damage. Dark magic is insidious and powerful, making Dark Mages dangerous opponents in prolonged encounters.

## Skills

### 1. Shadow Bolt
**Type:** Damage / Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 3 seconds  
**Description:** Fire a bolt of shadow energy that deals dark damage and applies a debuff reducing the target's damage by 10% for 15 seconds. Can stack up to 3 times.

### 2. Curse of Weakness
**Type:** Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Curse the target, reducing their attack power by 20% and increasing damage taken by 15% for 30 seconds. Can only affect one target at a time but is very powerful.

### 3. Shadow Drain
**Type:** Damage / Self-Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Drain life from the target, dealing dark damage over 5 seconds and healing yourself for 50% of damage dealt. Channeled ability that requires line of sight.

### 4. Shadow Form
**Type:** Transformation / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a being of shadow for 20 seconds. While transformed, you deal 30% more damage, take 25% less damage, and can pass through enemies. Ultimate defensive/offensive form.

### 5. Void Rift
**Type:** Ultimate Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Open a rift to the void at target location, creating an area of pure darkness that deals extreme dark damage to all enemies within for 8 seconds. Enemies caught in the rift are slowed by 60% and cannot see beyond the void.
