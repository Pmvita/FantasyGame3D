# Warlock

**Role:** Demon Summoner / Damage over Time  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Warlocks are practitioners of demonic magic who form pacts with otherworldly entities, gaining powerful abilities at a cost. These dark casters excel at summoning demonic allies and dealing sustained damage through curses and shadow magic. Warlock magic is powerful but comes with risks, making them high-risk, high-reward casters.

## Skills

### 1. Summon Imp
**Type:** Pet Summon  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a demonic imp that fights alongside you. The imp deals fire damage and can be sacrificed to restore 30% mana. Different demons offer different abilities.

### 2. Life Drain
**Type:** Damage / Self-Heal / Channel  
**Mana Cost:** 35 per second  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Channel dark energy that drains life from the target, dealing damage and healing yourself for 50% of damage dealt. Can be channeled for up to 5 seconds.

### 3. Soul Burn
**Type:** Damage over Time  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Burn the target's soul, dealing dark damage every second for 20 seconds. Each tick increases in damage. Can stack up to 2 times on the same target.

### 4. Demon Form
**Type:** Transformation / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a demonic form for 25 seconds, increasing all damage by 35% and gaining access to powerful demonic abilities. While transformed, take 15% more damage but deal significantly more.

### 5. Infernal Summon
**Type:** Ultimate Pet / Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a powerful infernal demon that fights for you for 60 seconds. The infernal deals massive fire damage and can tank enemies. When summoned, creates an explosion dealing area damage.
