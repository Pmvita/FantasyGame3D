# Wind Mage

**Role:** Aeromancy Specialist / Mobility  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Wind Mages harness the power of air and wind, specializing in mobility, crowd control, and rapid attacks. These aeromancers excel at positioning, using wind magic to move quickly and control enemy movement. Wind magic is fast and fluid, making Wind Mages highly mobile casters.

## Skills

### 1. Tornado
**Type:** Area Damage / Crowd Control  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 35 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a tornado that travels forward, lifting enemies into the air and dealing continuous wind damage for 6 seconds. Affected enemies cannot act while in the tornado.

### 2. Wind Blast
**Type:** Damage / Knockback  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Fire a powerful gust of wind that deals damage and pushes enemies back. Can be used to interrupt channeled abilities and create distance from melee attackers.

### 3. Wind Walk
**Type:** Mobility / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Become one with the wind for 15 seconds, increasing movement speed by 60% and allowing you to pass through enemies. Attacks cannot be interrupted while active.

### 4. Cyclone Shield
**Type:** Defensive / Reflective  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 50 seconds  
**Description:** Surround yourself with a swirling cyclone that deflects projectiles and reduces incoming ranged damage by 50% for 20 seconds. Melee attackers take wind damage when hitting the shield.

### 5. Hurricane
**Type:** Ultimate Area Control  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Create a massive hurricane at your location that deals extreme wind damage to all enemies within a large radius for 10 seconds. Enemies are continuously pushed to the edges and slowed by 70%.
