# Chronomancer

**Role:** Time Magic Specialist / Control Mage  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Chronomancers are mages who have mastered the manipulation of time itself, using temporal magic to control the flow of battle. These time-wielding casters excel at slowing enemies, speeding allies, and even reversing time itself. Chronomancers are incredibly powerful support casters who can change the outcome of battles through temporal manipulation.

## Skills

### 1. Time Slow
**Type:** Crowd Control / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Create a field that slows all enemies within by 70% for 15 seconds. Enemy abilities also take longer to cast. The field persists in the location where it was cast.

### 2. Haste Field
**Type:** Speed Buff / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 55  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create a field that speeds up all allies within, increasing movement speed by 50% and attack speed by 40% for 20 seconds. The field follows you or can be placed at a location.

### 3. Time Rewind
**Type:** Reversal / Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Rewind time for yourself or an ally, restoring them to their state 10 seconds ago (health, position, cooldowns). Can undo damage, death, or mistakes. Cannot rewind further than 10 seconds.

### 4. Temporal Shield
**Type:** Defensive / Time Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Create a temporal shield that causes incoming attacks to phase through you for 8 seconds. While active, 75% of damage is redirected to 3 seconds in the future, effectively delaying damage. Ultimate temporal defense.

### 5. Stop Time
**Type:** Ultimate Crowd Control  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Stop time for all enemies in a large area for 10 seconds. Frozen enemies cannot act and take 200% more damage. You and your allies can act normally. Ultimate time control.
