# Witch

**Role:** Hex Specialist / Debuff Caster  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Witches are masters of hexes and curses, specializing in weakening enemies and controlling the battlefield through debilitation. These spellcasters excel at support through enemy debuffs rather than direct damage. Witchcraft is subtle but powerful, making Witches valuable in group content by reducing enemy effectiveness.

## Skills

### 1. Hex of Weakness
**Type:** Debuff / Damage Reduction  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Hex the target, reducing their damage output by 25% and movement speed by 30% for 20 seconds. Multiple hexes can stack with different effects.

### 2. Voodoo Doll
**Type:** Damage Link / Debuff  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds  
**Description:** Create a voodoo link to the target, causing 30% of all damage you deal to be reflected onto them for 30 seconds. Also reveals their position through walls.

### 3. Curse of Misfortune
**Type:** Debuff / Critical Failure  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Curse the target with misfortune, causing their attacks to have a 25% chance to fail completely for 25 seconds. Extremely powerful against damage dealers.

### 4. Cauldron's Bounty
**Type:** Support / Buff Ally  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Create a magical cauldron that provides various beneficial effects to allies who interact with it. Can grant damage buffs, healing over time, or mana regeneration for 30 seconds.

### 5. Witching Hour
**Type:** Ultimate Debuff Area  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Create an area of pure hex magic that curses all enemies within, applying all your hex effects simultaneously and dealing dark damage over 15 seconds. Enemies are heavily debilitated while in the area.
