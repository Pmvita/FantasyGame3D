# Wizard

**Role:** Pure Arcane Specialist / Versatile Caster  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Wizards are masters of pure arcane magic, focusing on raw magical power and versatility. These arcane specialists excel at dealing consistent damage with powerful arcane spells. Wizards have access to the most diverse spell arsenal, making them adaptable to any situation through pure magical mastery.

## Skills

### 1. Arcane Blast
**Type:** High Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 4 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a blast of pure arcane energy that deals significant arcane damage. Each cast increases the damage of the next Arcane Blast by 20%, stacking up to 5 times.

### 2. Arcane Intellect
**Type:** Buff / Mana Management  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enhance your magical capabilities, increasing spell damage by 25% and mana regeneration by 100% for 30 seconds. Essential cooldown for sustained DPS.

### 3. Polymorph
**Type:** Crowd Control / Transform  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Transform an enemy into a harmless creature for 8 seconds, removing them from combat. The effect breaks if the target takes damage. Powerful single-target control.

### 4. Arcane Missile Barrage
**Type:** Channeled Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 50 per second  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Channel a continuous barrage of arcane missiles for up to 5 seconds, dealing high sustained damage. Can be moved while channeling. Mana efficient for damage output.

### 5. Time Warp
**Type:** Ultimate Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Manipulate time for all party members within 30 meters, resetting all cooldowns and restoring 50% mana instantly. Ultimate support ability for difficult encounters.
