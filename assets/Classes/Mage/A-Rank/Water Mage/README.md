# Water Mage

**Role:** Hydro Magic Specialist / Control  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Water Mages command the fluid forces of nature, using water magic for both damage and battlefield control. These hydromancers excel at slowing enemies, creating defensive barriers, and dealing sustained damage. Water magic is versatile, offering both offensive and defensive capabilities.

## Skills

### 1. Tidal Wave
**Type:** Area Damage / Knockback  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a massive tidal wave that pushes enemies back and deals water damage. Enemies caught in the wave are slowed by 50% for 5 seconds after impact.

### 2. Freeze
**Type:** Crowd Control / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Freeze the target solid, completely immobilizing them for 4 seconds and dealing water damage over time. Frozen enemies take 50% more damage from physical attacks.

### 3. Water Barrier
**Type:** Defensive / Healing  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds  
**Description:** Create a barrier of swirling water around yourself that reduces incoming damage by 30% and slowly restores mana over 15 seconds. Can be placed on allies.

### 4. Whirlpool
**Type:** Area Damage / Pull  
**Mana Cost:** 55  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Create a whirlpool at target location that pulls nearby enemies toward the center and deals continuous water damage for 6 seconds. Strong control ability.

### 5. Tsunami
**Type:** Ultimate Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 150 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a devastating tsunami that travels forward, dealing massive water damage to all enemies in its path. The wave continues for 50 meters, making it excellent for large groups of enemies.
