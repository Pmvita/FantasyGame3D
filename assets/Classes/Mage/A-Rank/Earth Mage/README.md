# Earth Mage

**Role:** Geomancy Specialist / Tanky Caster  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Earth Mages channel the power of the earth, combining defensive capabilities with powerful area damage. These geomancers are the tankiest of mage specializations, using earth magic to protect themselves while dealing consistent damage. Earth magic is solid and reliable, making Earth Mages durable casters.

## Skills

### 1. Earthquake
**Type:** Area Damage / Stun  
**Mana Cost:** 55  
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds  
**Description:** Cause the ground to shake in a large area, dealing earth damage and stunning all enemies for 3 seconds. The ground continues to tremble, dealing damage over time for 5 seconds after.

### 2. Stone Skin
**Type:** Defensive Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Transform your skin to stone for 30 seconds, increasing physical defense by 50% and reducing damage taken by 25%. Movement speed reduced by 20% while active.

### 3. Rock Spike
**Type:** Damage / Knockup  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds  
**Description:** Cause a spike of rock to erupt from the ground beneath the target, dealing earth damage and launching them into the air. High single-target damage.

### 4. Earth Wall
**Type:** Defensive Barrier / Obstacle  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Raise a wall of earth that blocks enemy movement and projectiles for 15 seconds. The wall can be used to protect allies or block enemy paths. Can be destroyed with enough damage.

### 5. Meteoric Impact
**Type:** Ultimate Single Target  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 150 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a massive boulder that crashes down on the target location after 3 seconds, dealing extreme earth damage in a large area and stunning all enemies for 5 seconds. Devastating finishing move.
