# Light Mage

**Role:** Lumomancy Specialist / Support DPS  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Light Mages wield the pure power of light and holy energy, dealing radiant damage while providing support to allies. These luminomancers excel at fighting undead and demons while offering utility to their party. Light magic combines offense with subtle support, making Light Mages valuable hybrid casters.

## Skills

### 1. Holy Light Beam
**Type:** Channeled Damage / Heal  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Channel a beam of holy light that deals radiant damage to enemies and heals nearby allies for 5 seconds. Deals 200% increased damage to undead and demons.

### 2. Blinding Flash
**Type:** Crowd Control / Area  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Release a flash of blinding light that stuns all enemies in a cone in front of you for 3 seconds. Undead enemies are stunned for 5 seconds instead.

### 3. Radiant Aura
**Type:** Area Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Emit a radiant aura for 30 seconds that increases all nearby allies' damage by 10% and reduces damage taken by 10%. Also provides passive healing over time to allies.

### 4. Purifying Light
**Type:** Area Cleanse / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 45  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Create an area of purifying light that removes all debuffs from allies and deals light damage to enemies every 2 seconds. The area lasts 10 seconds.

### 5. Divine Judgment
**Type:** Ultimate Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Call down divine judgment on a target area, dealing massive light damage to all enemies. Undead and demonic enemies take 300% increased damage and are instantly destroyed if below 30% health.
