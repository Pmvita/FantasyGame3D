# Fire Mage

**Role:** Pyromancy Specialist / Burst Damage  
**Rank:** A-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Fire Mages specialize in the destructive power of flame, dealing explosive damage and area-of-effect devastation. These pyromancers excel at burst damage and burning enemies over time. Fire magic is aggressive and powerful, making Fire Mages one of the highest damage-dealing specializations.

## Skills

### 1. Meteor Strike
**Type:** Area Damage / Channel  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Summon a meteor from the sky after a 3-second channel, dealing massive fire damage in a large area and leaving a burning ground effect that damages enemies for 10 seconds.

### 2. Flame Burst
**Type:** Instant Damage / Spread  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds  
**Description:** Unleash a burst of flame that deals fire damage to the target and spreads to nearby enemies. Each enemy hit spreads the flame to 2 additional nearby enemies.

### 3. Combustion
**Type:** Damage over Time / Amplify  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Ignite the target, causing fire damage every second for 15 seconds. Each tick of damage increases in intensity. Can stack up to 3 times on the same target.

### 4. Fire Shield
**Type:** Defensive / Damage Reflection  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Surround yourself with flames for 20 seconds. Enemies that attack you take fire damage equal to 30% of the damage they deal. Also provides 15% damage reduction.

### 5. Inferno
**Type:** Ultimate Area Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Create a massive inferno at your location that deals extreme fire damage to all enemies in a large area for 8 seconds. You are immune to your own fire damage while channeling.
