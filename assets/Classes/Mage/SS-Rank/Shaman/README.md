# Shaman

**Role:** Elemental Shaman / Nature Mage  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Shamans are mages who commune with nature and the elements, wielding all four elements in harmony. These nature-connected casters excel at versatile elemental magic and support through totems. Shamans balance all elements, making them adaptable to any situation.

## Skills

### 1. Elemental Totems
**Type:** Utility / Support  
**Mana Cost:** 40 per totem  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Place totems that provide various effects: Fire (damage), Water (healing), Earth (defense), Wind (speed). Up to 4 totems can be active. Totems persist for 60 seconds or until destroyed.

### 2. Elemental Mastery
**Type:** Buff / Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Harmonize all elements for 45 seconds, allowing you to cast any elemental spell regardless of specialization. All elemental damage is increased by 50% and costs 30% less mana.

### 3. Chain Lightning
**Type:** Chain Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Cast lightning that jumps between up to 10 enemies, dealing increasing damage with each jump. Each enemy hit increases the damage of the next jump by 20%. Powerful multi-target ability.

### 4. Spirit Walk
**Type:** Mobility / Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a spirit for 15 seconds, allowing you to move through enemies and walls. While in spirit form, you cannot attack but are immune to all damage and can resurrect at your corpse if killed.

### 5. Elemental Fusion
**Type:** Ultimate Combination  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Combine all four elements into a devastating attack, dealing massive damage of all types simultaneously. Creates a massive area effect that deals fire, water, wind, and earth damage for 20 seconds. Ultimate elemental power.
