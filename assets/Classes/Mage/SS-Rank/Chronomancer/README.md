# Chronomancer

**Role:** Master Time Manipulator / Cooldown Controller  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Chronomancer A-Rank path)

## Description

Chronomancers at the SS-Rank have achieved absolute mastery over time itself, manipulating the very flow of temporal energy. These masters of time excel at altering fight pacing through cooldown manipulation and time dilation. Chronomancers require high cognitive load as they constantly manage multiple timelines and temporal effects simultaneously. Their power to control time makes them incredibly valuable in any encounter.

## Skills

### 1. Temporal Manipulation
**Type:** Cooldown Control / Time Rewind  
**Mana Cost:** 80  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Rewind time for yourself or an ally, resetting all their ability cooldowns to their state 15 seconds ago. Can also fast-forward cooldowns, reducing remaining cooldown time by 50%. Ultimate cooldown management tool.

### 2. Time Dilation Field
**Type:** Area Control / Speed Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 90  
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds  
**Description:** Create a field that slows enemy time by 80% and speeds ally time by 50% for 20 seconds. Enemies move and attack incredibly slowly while allies move and attack faster. The field persists at its location. High cognitive load to manage optimally.

### 3. Paradox Shield
**Type:** Defensive / Time Loop  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Create a temporal shield that causes incoming damage to loop back 5 seconds in time, effectively negating it. The shield can absorb unlimited damage but only works for 10 seconds. Damage absorbed is delayed, not prevented.

### 4. Cooldown Acceleration
**Type:** Ally Buff / Resource Management  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Accelerate time for an ally, reducing all their ability cooldowns by 75% for 30 seconds. The ally's abilities recharge almost instantly during this time. Can be used on multiple allies but requires careful mana management.

### 5. Temporal Stasis
**Type:** Ultimate Time Control  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Completely stop time for all enemies in a massive area for 15 seconds. Frozen enemies cannot act, are immune to damage but take 300% damage when time resumes. You and your allies can act normally. Can manipulate enemy positions during stasis. Ultimate time mastery.
