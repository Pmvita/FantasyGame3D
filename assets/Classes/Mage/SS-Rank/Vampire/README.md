# Vampire

**Role:** Blood Mage / Life Stealer  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Vampires are mages who have embraced the power of blood magic, using life force as both weapon and resource. These immortal casters excel at draining life from enemies to sustain themselves and deal damage. Vampires are powerful but require constant life drain to maintain their power.

## Skills

### 1. Blood Drain
**Type:** Damage / Self-Heal / Channel  
**Mana Cost:** 30 per second  
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds  
**Description:** Drain life from an enemy, dealing dark damage and healing yourself for 100% of damage dealt. Can be channeled for up to 8 seconds. Each second increases damage and healing by 25%.

### 2. Vampiric Form
**Type:** Transformation / Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into your true vampire form for 45 seconds. While transformed, gain 50% lifesteal on all attacks, move 40% faster, and can turn into a bat for brief flight. Damage taken from sunlight is increased.

### 3. Blood Mist
**Type:** Area Damage / Crowd Control  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Transform into a cloud of blood mist for 10 seconds, becoming immune to physical attacks and dealing dark damage to all enemies you pass through. Can move through enemies while in mist form.

### 4. Blood Bond
**Type:** Link / Damage Share  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Create a blood link with an enemy for 30 seconds. All damage you take is shared with the linked target (50/50 split), and all healing you receive also heals you for 25% more. Powerful tanking ability.

### 5. Immortal Coil
**Type:** Ultimate Life Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 150  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of true immortality for 30 seconds. While active, you cannot die (health cannot drop below 1), all damage dealt heals you for 200%, and killing enemies extends the duration by 5 seconds. Ultimate vampire power.
