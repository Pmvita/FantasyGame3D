# Card Caster

**Role:** Gambling Mage / RNG Specialist  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Card Casters are mages who channel magic through enchanted cards, using chance and fortune to unleash powerful effects. These mystical gamblers excel at versatile magic through their card deck system. Card Casters are unpredictable but powerful, with abilities that vary based on the cards drawn from their deck.

## Skills

### 1. Draw Cards
**Type:** Resource / Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 30  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Draw 3 cards from your deck. Cards can be damage, healing, buff, debuff, or utility. The cards available change based on your deck composition. Can hold up to 10 cards.

### 2. Play Card
**Type:** Variable Effect  
**Mana Cost:** Varies (20-60)  
**Cooldown:** 2 seconds  
**Description:** Play a card from your hand, activating its unique effect. Cards have different rarities and powers. Higher rarity cards have more powerful effects but are rarer to draw.

### 3. Card Combo
**Type:** Enhanced Effect  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Play multiple cards in sequence for enhanced effects. Combos can create powerful synergistic effects. The more cards in the combo, the more powerful the result.

### 4. Wild Card
**Type:** Random Powerful Effect  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Play a wild card that has a completely random powerful effect. Can be anything from massive damage to full party healing to enemy transformation. Ultimate gamble.

### 5. Deck Mastery
**Type:** Ultimate Control  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Manipulate probability itself, allowing you to draw specific cards from your deck for 60 seconds. During this time, you can control what cards you draw, making all combos possible. Ultimate card control.
