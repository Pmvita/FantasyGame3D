# Spell Master

**Role:** Ultimate Spellcaster / Versatile Mage  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Wizard path)

## Description

Spell Masters are mages who have mastered every school of magic, wielding an extensive arsenal of spells. These ultimate casters excel at versatility, having access to spells from all magical disciplines. Spell Masters are the most versatile mages, capable of adapting to any situation through their vast knowledge.

## Skills

### 1. Spell Library
**Type:** Spell Access / Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Access your spell library, allowing you to learn and use spells from any magical school. You can switch between different spell sets based on the situation. Up to 50 spells can be prepared at once.

### 2. Quick Cast
**Type:** Cast Time Reduction  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enter a state of rapid casting for 30 seconds, reducing all cast times by 75% and allowing instant casting of normally channeled spells. Spell damage is increased by 25% during this time.

### 3. Spell Weaving
**Type:** Combination Casting  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
**Description:** Combine multiple spells into a single more powerful effect. Can combine up to 3 spells, with the combined spell having effects from all components. Creates unique powerful effects.

### 4. Counterspell
**Type:** Interrupt / Reflect  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 24 seconds  
**Description:** Counter an enemy spell, preventing its cast and reflecting it back to the caster for 150% damage. Can counter any spell type. Works even against normally uncounterable spells.

### 5. Master of All Magic
**Type:** Ultimate Versatility  
**Mana Cost:** 100  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Achieve perfect mastery of all magic for 60 seconds. During this time, all spells cost no mana, have no cooldowns, and deal 100% more damage. You can cast any spell instantly. Ultimate magical power.
