# Illusionist

**Role:** Deception Master / Control Specialist  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage

## Description

Illusionists are mages who have mastered the art of deception and mind control, using illusions to confuse and control enemies. These masters of misdirection excel at battlefield control through confusion and trickery. Illusionists can make enemies fight each other and see things that aren't there.

## Skills

### 1. Mirror Image
**Type:** Decoy / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds  
**Description:** Create perfect copies of yourself that enemies cannot distinguish from the real you. Mirrors can cast spells and deal 40% of your damage. When destroyed, they explode for area damage. Up to 5 mirrors can exist.

### 2. Mass Confusion
**Type:** Crowd Control / Chaos  
**Mana Cost:** 70  
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds  
**Description:** Confuse all enemies in a large area, causing them to attack each other for 15 seconds. Confused enemies deal 50% less damage to you and your allies but normal damage to each other.

### 3. Invisibility
**Type:** Stealth / Utility  
**Mana Cost:** 60  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Become completely invisible for 30 seconds or until you attack. While invisible, you can cast spells without breaking invisibility. Enemies cannot see or target you.

### 4. Hallucination
**Type:** Debuff / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 55  
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds  
**Description:** Cause an enemy to hallucinate, making them see hostile illusions for 20 seconds. The illusions attack the target, dealing real damage. The target cannot distinguish illusions from reality.

### 5. Grand Illusion
**Type:** Ultimate Reality Manipulation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Create a massive illusion that affects all enemies in a huge area. Enemies see themselves fighting each other, take damage from illusions, and cannot target real players for 25 seconds. Ultimate deception.
