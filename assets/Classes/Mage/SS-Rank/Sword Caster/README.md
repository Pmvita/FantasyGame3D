# Sword Caster

**Role:** Magical Swordsman / Hybrid Fighter  
**Rank:** SS-Rank  
**Base Class:** Mage (Battle Mage path)

## Description

Sword Casters are mages who have perfected the fusion of magic and melee combat, wielding enchanted blades with devastating spell-enhanced strikes. These hybrid fighters excel at close-range combat with magical enhancements. Sword Casters combine the best of both worlds, dealing both physical and magical damage simultaneously.

## Skills

### 1. Spellblade
**Type:** Weapon Enhancement  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
**Description:** Enchant your weapon with pure magic for 45 seconds, causing all attacks to deal equal physical and magical damage. Each attack has a chance to cast a random spell. Ultimate hybrid damage.

### 2. Arcane Slash
**Type:** Melee / Range Hybrid  
**Mana Cost:** 50  
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds  
**Description:** Perform a slash that extends 30 meters as an arcane wave. Deals both physical and magical damage to all enemies in the path. Can be used to attack multiple targets from melee range.

### 3. Blink Strike
**Type:** Mobility / Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Teleport to an enemy and strike them, dealing 300% weapon damage plus arcane damage. Can be used on any enemy within 40 meters. Leaves behind an arcane mark for quick return.

### 4. Mana Blade
**Type:** Resource Conversion  
**Mana Cost:** 0 (Uses mana as damage)  
**Cooldown:** None  
**Description:** Convert mana directly into blade damage. Each point of mana consumed adds to attack damage. Allows you to sacrifice mana for massive burst damage. Can be toggled on/off.

### 5. Ultimate Fusion: Spellsword
**Type:** Ultimate Transformation  
**Mana Cost:** 120  
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds  
**Description:** Become one with magic and blade for 60 seconds, transforming into a being of pure spell-sword energy. While transformed, all attacks are instant-cast spells, and all spells enhance your blade. Deal 150% more damage and take 40% less damage. Ultimate hybrid form.
