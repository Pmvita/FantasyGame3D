# Mage

**Role:** Arcane magic specialist.  
**Strengths:** Powerful elemental magic, ranged attacks, high burst damage.

**Differentiation:**  
- Ranged damage dealer with elemental spells
- Mana-based resource system
- Positioning and range management crucial
- Low physical defense, high magical output

## Description

The Mage is a master of arcane arts, wielding raw magical power to devastate enemies from afar. Mages specialize in elemental magic and spellcasting, delivering explosive area-of-effect damage and controlling the battlefield. While fragile in close combat, their mastery of magic makes them one of the most powerful damage dealers in the game, capable of turning the tide of battle with a single well-placed spell.

## Skills

### 1. Fireball
**Type:** Damage Spell  
**Mana Cost:** 25  
**Cooldown:** 2 seconds  
**Description:** Launch a blazing fireball at the target, dealing fire damage. Has a small area of effect, damaging nearby enemies.

### 2. Frostbolt
**Type:** Damage / Slow  
**Mana Cost:** 20  
**Cooldown:** 2.5 seconds  
**Description:** Cast a bolt of ice that deals frost damage and slows the target's movement speed by 40% for 5 seconds.

### 3. Arcane Missiles
**Type:** Channeled Damage  
**Mana Cost:** 30 per second  
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds  
**Description:** Channel magic missiles that rapidly hit the target for 3 seconds, dealing consistent arcane damage. Can be moved while channeling.

### 4. Teleport
**Type:** Mobility  
**Mana Cost:** 35  
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
**Description:** Instantly teleport 15 meters in the direction you're facing. Removes movement-impairing effects.

### 5. Arcane Shield
**Type:** Defensive Buff  
**Mana Cost:** 40  
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds  
**Description:** Create a protective barrier that absorbs damage equal to 30% of your maximum health. Lasts 15 seconds or until broken.
